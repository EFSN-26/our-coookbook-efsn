# Artichoke Pesto Pasta

## Description
A fun twist on regular pesto, this artichoke pesto pasta is made with frozen artichokes and walnuts, tossed with pasta for a simple side dish or main.

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

- 1 pound pasta, preferably rotini, farfalle or other short pasta
- 1 12-ounce package frozen artichoke hearts, thawed
- 1 cup fresh basil leaves, packed down
- 2/3 cup chopped walnuts
- 2 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
- 1 lemon, zest and juice
- 1/2 teaspoon salt and pepper
- 2/3 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1/2 cup fresh grated parmesan cheese*

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook until just al dente, reserving 1/2 cup water.
2. Place the artichoke hearts, basil, walnuts, garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in the base of a food processor. Process on low until roughly chopped, stopping a few times to scrape down the sides.
3. With the machine running, drizzle in the olive oil and process until well combined.
4. Add the pesto to a bowl and add in the warm pasta, and toss until just combined. Add in the cheese and toss together again until combined. If the mixture is too thick, add in a tablespoon or two of the pasta water to thin.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
*You can make this vegan by omitting the parmesan cheese, or add a tablespoon of nutritional yeast to the pesto mixture for a cheese-like taste.

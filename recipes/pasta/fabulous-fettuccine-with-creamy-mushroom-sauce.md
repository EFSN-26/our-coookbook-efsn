# Fabulous Fettuccine Recipe With Creamy Mushroom Sauce

## Description
Creamy and full of tender mushrooms, this easy fettuccine recipe will be a family pleaser. The pasta recipe takes about 25 minutes from start to finish — and it cooks in 20 minutes. Easy enough for a Monday night dinner and impressive enough for company on the weekend. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/35131/Fabulous-Fettuccine-Recipe-With-Creamy-Mushroom-Sauce-20-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 12 ounces fettuccine, cooked according to package directions
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 16 ounces mushrooms, chopped
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 cup white wine
- 2 cups heavy whipping cream
- 1/2 cup chicken broth
- 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
- salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Melt the butter in a skillet. Cook the mushrooms until tender and beginning to brown, about 5 to 6 minutes. Add the garlic and cook 30 seconds.
2. Pour in the wine and cook until it has almost evaporated.
3. Add the cream, chicken stock, basil and thyme. Season with salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until thickened, about 15 minutes.
4. Add the cooked fettuccine and toss. Serve with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- You could use penne, spaghetti noodles, linguine or your favorite pasta instead of fettuccine.
- Store the pasta in an airtight container in the fridge for up to four days.
- Add a side dish of garlic bread and a salad for a meal not soon forgotten.

# Creamy Spinach Artichoke Pasta Casserole

## Description
Family-pleasing bowtie (farfalle) pasta baked with spinach, artichoke hearts, cream and two cheeses. This vegetarian pasta casserole has minimal prep and bakes in about 30 minutes. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/18125/Creamy-Spinach-Artichoke-Pasta-Casserole-Recipe-Makes-Meatless-Monday-Magic-8-Ingredients-35-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 8 ounces bowtie pasta, cooked according to package directions for al dente
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 - 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 10 ounces fresh spinach or frozen chopped spinach (thawed and squeezed dry)
- 14 ounces (1 can) artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
- 2 cups heavy cream
- 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 1/2 cups grated mozzarella

## Instructions
1. In a skillet, heat the olive oil and cook the garlic about 1 minute. Add the spinach and cook until wilted, about 4 minutes. Add the chopped artichokes and heavy cream. Bring to a simmer.
2. Stir in the Parmesan and 1/2 cup of the mozzarella until cheeses have melted. Add the cooked pasta and season to taste with salt and pepper.
3. Pour the mixture into a casserole dish. Sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella cheese. Bake in a 400-degree F oven for about 20 minutes, or until cheese is melted and begins to brown.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
Any short pasta works — rigatoni, penne, ziti or fusilli are all good substitutes for bowtie. Store leftovers in the fridge for up to four days.

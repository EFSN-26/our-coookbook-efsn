# Creamy Lemon Ricotta Pasta

## Description
Lemon and pasta are a match made in pasta heaven. Add ricotta cheese and healthy baby spinach, and you've got a winning vegetarian dinner or side dish to go with your favorite protein — just four ingredients and on the table in under 20 minutes. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/68187/Creamy-Lemon-Ricotta-Pasta-Recipe-Is-Dinner-Done-Right-4-Ingredients-15-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 16 ounces pasta
- 16 ounces ricotta cheese
- 1 lemon, juiced and zested
- 2 cups fresh baby spinach
- salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Cook the pasta according to package directions for al dente. Drain, reserving 1 cup of the pasta water. Return to the pot.
2. Add the ricotta, baby spinach, lemon juice, lemon zest, and salt and pepper, to taste.
3. Stir in 1/2 cup of the reserved pasta water. Add more pasta water, if needed. Taste and add more salt and pepper, if needed.
4. Serve with freshly grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Don't overcook the pasta or you'll have a mushy dish, and don't forget to save the pasta water when draining.
- Any type of pasta works — the author used spaghetti noodles.
- Love spinach? Add more to the recipe; just stir after each addition until it wilts.
- Use a fine grater or microplane to grate the lemon zest.
- Serve as a main meatless dinner, or as a side dish with chicken, seafood, beef, lamb or pork.

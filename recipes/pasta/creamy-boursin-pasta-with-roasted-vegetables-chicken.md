# Creamy Boursin Pasta With Roasted Vegetables & Chicken

## Description
A viral TikTok-style Boursin pasta dressed up with roasted grape tomatoes, zucchini, asparagus, mushrooms and rotisserie chicken. The whole round of Boursin cheese roasts right on the sheet pan with the vegetables, then gets stirred into the cooked pasta with a splash of starchy pasta water for a creamy sauce. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/54067/Creamy-Boursin-Pasta-Recipe-With-Roasted-Vegetables-Chicken-Is-High-Protein-High-Fiber-Heaven)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- olive oil, for drizzling
- 2 cups grape tomatoes
- 1 zucchini, chopped
- 1 bunch asparagus, chopped (approximately 1 pound)
- 8 ounces fresh mushrooms, sliced
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 6 big cloves fresh garlic, peeled and halved
- 5.2 ounces (1 box) Boursin cheese
- 4 ounces cavatappi pasta, or your favorite pasta (half a box if using a standard box)
- 1/2 a rotisserie chicken, deboned and shredded or chopped
- 1 bunch basil, rough chopped (about 1 cup, or to taste)
- kosher salt and cracked black pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Put the tomatoes, zucchini, asparagus, mushrooms, onion and garlic onto a large sheet pan. Drizzle generously with olive oil, kosher salt and lots of cracked black pepper. Put the whole round of Boursin cheese in the center of the pan. Drizzle with more olive oil and black pepper. Bake in a preheated 400-degree F oven for about 30 minutes.
2. Cook the cavatappi according to package directions. Drain, reserving 1/2 to 1 cup of the pasta water.
3. Transfer the roasted vegetables and Boursin cheese to the pot with the pasta. Add the chicken and 1/2 to 1 cup of the pasta water (chicken broth can be substituted). Mix until creamy. Top with the basil.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
For a vegetarian version, leave the chicken out. Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge and reheat in the microwave. The recipe author noted she'd use less pasta next time to favor the vegetables.

# Quick Italian Sausage Pasta with Tomato Cream Sauce

## Description
A quick, budget-friendly weeknight dinner: bowtie pasta and browned Italian sausage tossed in a creamy tomato sauce with Parmesan. Only seven ingredients and about 20 minutes start to finish. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/101257/Quick-Italian-Sausage-Pasta-Recipe-With-Tomato-Cream-Sauce-7-Ingredients-20-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 5 servings)_

- 8 ounces bowtie pasta (or another short pasta)
- 1 pound Italian sausage, bulk or links (cut into bite-sized pieces if links)
- 1 small onion, chopped (optional)
- 14 ounces canned diced tomatoes (1 can), undrained
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 cup evaporated milk, whole milk, or heavy cream
- 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

## Instructions
1. Cook the pasta in salted boiling water according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. While the pasta cooks, put the sausage into a Dutch oven or large pot. Add the onion, if using.
3. Cook, stirring often, until the sausage is browned and cooked through, draining off excess fat if needed.
4. Add the diced tomatoes with their liquid, the oregano, garlic powder, and milk to the pot.
5. Stir to combine and cook, stirring occasionally, for about 5 minutes, until the sauce thickens.
6. Add the cooked pasta to the pot.
7. Stir in the Parmesan cheese.
8. Cook for about 2 minutes to heat through.
9. Serve with more grated Parmesan and garnish with fresh oregano, if desired.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- No bowties on hand? Try penne, rotini, or fusilli.
- Pork or chicken Italian sausage both work.
- Store leftovers in an airtight container and reheat in the microwave.
- Round out the meal with a side of vegetables, garlic bread, or salad.

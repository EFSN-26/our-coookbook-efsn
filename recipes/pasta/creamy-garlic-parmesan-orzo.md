# Creamy Garlic Parmesan Orzo

## Description
Pasta isn't just for main dishes — this garlic Parmesan orzo recipe is buttery, creamy and cooks up in no time, making it a welcome change of pace for the side dish rotation.

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 2 cups uncooked orzo, cooked according to package directions
- 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
- 6 tablespoons butter
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1/4 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley or basil
- lemon zest, for garnish (optional)

## Instructions
1. Melt the butter in a large skillet. Add the garlic and cook 30 seconds.
2. Add the cooked orzo, cheese, milk, parsley or basil, and salt and pepper, to taste. Stir and heat through.
3. Garnish with fresh lemon zest and basil or parsley leaves. Store the orzo in an airtight container in the fridge; reheat in the microwave.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
Love lemon? Stir in some lemon juice and garnish with lemon zest. If you don't have orzo, try ditalini, elbow macaroni, couscous, quinoa or arborio rice instead. Serve with chicken, pork, beef or seafood, or eat it as a vegetarian lunch or dinner.

# 5-Minute Greek Salad (Maroulosalata)

## Description
Maroulosalata is a Greek lettuce salad — romaine, fresh dill and scallions, feta, lemon, and olive oil — that keeps ingredients and prep to a minimum while staying true to a classic Greek salad's crisp, bright flavor. [Source](https://www.aol.com/5-minute-greek-salad-cant-000100527.html)

## Ingredients
_(for 4-6 servings)_

- 1 English cucumber, quartered lengthwise and thinly sliced
- 2 scallions, thinly sliced
- 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh dill
- 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, plus more to taste
- 4 ounces feta cheese
- 1 lemon
- 2 hearts romaine lettuce, roughly chopped
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- Salt, to taste

## Instructions
1. Add the cucumber to a large bowl with the scallions, dill, and black pepper.
2. Use your hands to crumble the feta cheese over the top.
3. Halve the lemon and juice half of it into the bowl, tossing the ingredients to combine.
4. Add the shredded lettuce to the bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and toss to combine.
5. Taste and season with salt, more black pepper, and lemon juice as desired before serving.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Easy variations: add another lettuce like arugula, spinach, or watercress; use smaller, sweeter Persian cucumbers instead of English; use a mix of fresh dill, mint, and parsley; add sliced red onion, tomatoes, or bell pepper; finish with a splash of red wine vinegar or a sprinkle of dried oregano; toss in shredded or cubed chicken, chickpeas, or toasted breadcrumbs to make it heartier.

# Balsamic Spinach Strawberry Feta Salad with Walnuts

## Description
Fresh spinach, strawberries, feta, and walnuts tossed with a simple balsamic vinaigrette. It can be meal-prepped by keeping the salad ingredients and dressing in separate containers until ready to serve.

## Ingredients
_(for 3 servings)_

- 1.8 oz baby spinach
- 12 piece strawberries
- 3.5 oz feta cheese
- 1/2 cup walnuts
- 3 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin
- 1.5 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, ground

## Instructions
1. Rinse the baby spinach leaves well and pat dry.
2. Rinse the strawberries, pat dry, and cut off the stem part. Cut into halves, quarters, or slices.
3. Take the feta cheese from the package and get rid of excess cheese water. Cut into small cubes or crumble into the salad just before serving.
4. Crush the walnuts slightly or cut into halves so they mix in easily.
5. Combine all the salad ingredients in medium bowls.
6. For the dressing, mix together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, mustard, and black pepper with a fork or whisk until well combined.
7. Pour the dressing over the salad just before serving.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
The salad is great served cooled — keep the salad ingredients and dressing in the fridge separately until serving time. Optional additions: avocado, bacon, boiled egg, pears, red onion, candied walnuts, dried cranberries, or fresh blueberries.

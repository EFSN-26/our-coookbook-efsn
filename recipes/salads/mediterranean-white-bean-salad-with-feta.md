# Mediterranean White Bean Salad with Feta

## Description
Cannellini beans marinated in a tangy lemon vinaigrette with diced red bell pepper, red onion and parsley, then topped with crumbled feta — an easy, make-ahead Greek-inspired side salad. [Source](https://immigrantstable.com/mediterranean-white-bean-salad-with-feta/)

Note: total time below includes a 30-minute marinating/chilling period after the salad is assembled.

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

- 1 can white cannellini beans (or 1 cup dried cannellini beans, cooked)
- 1/2 red pepper, diced finely
- 1/2 red onion, sliced thinly
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- juice of 1 lemon
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- salt & pepper, to taste
- 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
- 1/4 cup parsley
- 1/2 cup feta cheese (or 100 g)

## Instructions
1. If using canned beans, rinse and drain them. If making beans from scratch, soak the white beans overnight and cook as needed — on the stovetop or in an Instant Pot.
2. Dice the vegetables finely.
3. To make the dressing, squeeze the juice from 1 lemon into a bowl. Add 3 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil, the minced garlic, about 1/2 teaspoon of salt, and 1/4 teaspoon of freshly ground black pepper. Add 1 teaspoon of honey, if desired. Whisk together until fully combined. Taste and adjust seasoning.
4. In a large salad bowl, add the beans, bell pepper, onions and parsley, and stir. Add the feta cheese and stir gently to combine.
5. Refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving to let the flavors marinate.
6. Serve with crusty bread and additional olive oil for extra punch.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Cannellini beans are recommended for their creamy texture and nutty flavor; Great Northern beans, borlotti beans or navy beans also work.
- This salad keeps well for meal prep — it will keep in the fridge for 4-5 days if you add the feta, tomatoes and herbs only when serving (rather than in advance).
- Use a good-quality, fruity extra virgin olive oil, and a Greek feta made from goat/sheep's milk blended with cow's milk for best flavor.
- Great alongside picnics, lunch boxes, dinner parties, or as a light accompaniment to roasted meats or fish.

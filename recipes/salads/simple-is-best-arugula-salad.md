# Simple Is Best Arugula Salad

## Description
A clean, simple arugula salad with toasted almonds, shaved Pecorino cheese and crisp apples, tossed in a bright lemon-Dijon vinaigrette — three main ingredients plus an easy dressing you'll want to make again and again. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/72156/Simple-Is-Best-Arugula-Salad-Recipe-With-Apples-Nuts-Tangy-Vinaigrette)

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

Salad:
- 6 ounces arugula
- 1/2 cup slivered almonds, toasted
- 4 ounces shaved Pecorino cheese
- 1 - 2 apples, washed, cored and sliced into bite-size pieces

Salad Dressing:
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1/2 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- 1/4 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/4 teaspoon salt (or to taste)
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper (or to taste)
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 2 teaspoons maple syrup (optional)

## Instructions
1. Make the dressing by placing all the dressing ingredients in a small bowl or jar and blend well.
2. Place the salad greens in a large bowl and pour 1/3 of the dressing over it. Toss well.
3. Add the nuts, apples and cheese and a bit more dressing. Toss again. Serve in salad bowls and enjoy!

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Instead of almonds try cashews, pine nuts, walnuts, pecans or even pumpkin seeds.
- Instead of Pecorino, you could use Parmesan or your favorite cheese.
- Fresh is always best with lemon juice, so squeeze your own if you can; bottled lemon juice works in a pinch.

# Dill Pickle Ranch Potato Salad

## Description
Tender Yukon Gold potatoes tossed with a creamy ranch dressing made from fresh herbs, garlic and pickle brine. Crisp celery, red onion and chopped dill pickles add crunch and a bright, tangy finish. [Source](https://www.allrecipes.com/dill-pickle-ranch-potato-salad-recipe-12011343)

## Ingredients
_(for 12 servings)_

- 6 large eggs
- 2 pounds Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 2/3 cup mayonnaise
- 2/3 cup sour cream
- 3 tablespoons pickle brine, from the jar
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 cup thinly sliced celery
- 1 cup chopped dill pickles
- 1/4 cup finely chopped red onion

## Instructions
1. Place eggs in a single layer in a large saucepan. Add enough cold water to cover eggs by 1 inch. Bring to a rapid boil over high heat. Remove from heat, cover and let stand 15 minutes; drain. Place eggs in ice water until cool enough to handle; drain. Peel and coarsely chop.
2. Meanwhile, place potatoes in a large saucepan with enough salted water to cover. Bring to boiling, reduce heat and simmer, covered, until just tender, about 10 minutes; drain. Spread on a baking sheet or tray and let cool slightly, about 5 minutes.
3. Meanwhile, for dressing, in a large bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, pickle brine, dill, parsley, chives, garlic, salt and pepper.
4. Add eggs, potatoes, celery, pickles, and onion to the bowl; toss to combine.
5. Cover and chill 1 to 2 hours before serving.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
One reviewer substituted 1/2 cup dill relish for the chopped dill pickles when they didn't have any on hand. Another reviewer used dried dill and parsley at a 1 teaspoon dried per 1 tablespoon fresh ratio, and substituted Miracle Whip for the mayonnaise.

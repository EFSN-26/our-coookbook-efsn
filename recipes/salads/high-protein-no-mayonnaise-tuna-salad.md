# High-Protein No-Mayonnaise Tuna Salad

## Description
A mayonnaise-free tuna salad built on green olives, a touch of brown sugar, olive oil, apple cider vinegar, red pepper flakes, garlic powder, and smoked paprika — full of flavor without the mayo, and ready in 10 minutes. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/19074/High-Protein-No-Mayonnaise-Tuna-Salad-Recipe-8-Ingredients-10-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 3 servings)_

- 10 ounces (2 cans) tuna in water, drained
- 1/4 cup chopped green olives
- 2 teaspoons brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 2 teaspoons apple cider vinegar
- 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes or ground cayenne pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika

## Instructions
1. Combine all the ingredients in a bowl. Mix well. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
2. Cover the tuna and allow the flavors to blend for about 10 minutes. Serve on bread with desired toppings, or store in an airtight container in the fridge.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Not an olive fan? Chopped red bell pepper, pickles, green or red onions, or chopped cucumber are good substitutes.
- Canned salmon can be used in place of the canned tuna.
- Fresh herbs can be added for extra flavor.
- Serve as a sandwich, wrap, or piled onto lettuce and other salad fixings.
- Nutrition per serving (per source): 139 calories, 5g fat, 1g saturated fat, 4g carbs, 1g fiber, 3g net carbs, 3g sugar, 18g protein, 384mg sodium, 34mg cholesterol.

# Mediterranean Lemony Lentil Salad

## Description
A filling, fresh, and nutritious lentil salad tossed with a bright lemon-garlic dressing, crunchy cucumber and bell peppers, green onions, and fresh herbs. Comes together in just 30 minutes and works as a vegetarian main dish or a side.

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings)_

### Dressing
- 2 large lemons, juiced
- 1-2 large garlic cloves, pressed or minced
- 1/2 - 1 teaspoon Urfa biber, Aleppo pepper, or crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
- 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
- salt and black pepper, to taste

### Lentil Salad
- 1 cup black or green lentils, rinsed
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 English cucumber, cut into large pieces
- 1 red bell pepper (fresh or roasted), cored and chopped
- 1 orange or yellow bell pepper, cored and chopped
- 2 green onions, trimmed and chopped
- 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley leaves
- 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint leaves

## Instructions
1. Cook the lentils with the bay leaf in 3 cups of water. Bring to a boil on high heat, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes, until soft but not mushy. (You can also skip this step and use precooked lentils.) Drain excess water and place lentils into a large serving bowl. Let cool for 10 minutes before adding vegetables and herbs.
2. Make the dressing by placing the dressing ingredients in a bowl. Season with salt and black pepper.
3. Use a fork or whisk to combine. Taste and add more seasonings, if desired.
4. Top the lentils with the dressing and toss well to coat. Add the vegetables and herbs and toss well.
5. Taste to see if additional seasonings are needed and enjoy!

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Urfa biber is a dried Turkish chili pepper with a smoky, raisin-like flavor; substitute crushed red pepper flakes or Aleppo pepper if unavailable.
- The salad shown in the source's photos was a double recipe.
- Feta cheese crumbles are a lovely addition, per the author.
- Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge for up to three days.
- Serve as a vegetarian main dish or as a side dish with dinner.

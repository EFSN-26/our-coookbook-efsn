# High-Protein Caprese Salad

## Description
A classic Caprese salad of vine-ripe tomatoes, fresh mozzarella and basil, said to have originated on the Italian island of Capri, with its green-white-red layers echoing the Italian flag. This version is finished with balsamic glaze and black pepper. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/89580/High-Protein-Caprese-Salad-Recipe-Is-Summers-Perfect-Salad-5-Ingredients-5-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 2 servings)_

- 1 large tomato (about 1 1/4 cups), sliced
- 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
- 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, whole or torn (plus additional for garnish, if desired)
- 2 teaspoons balsamic glaze, or to taste
- 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, or to taste

## Instructions
1. On a medium to large plate, layer the tomatoes, mozzarella and basil leaves.
2. Drizzle the balsamic glaze evenly over the top of the salad.
3. Top with the pepper and additional basil, as garnish, if desired. Serve immediately.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- There's debate about how to dress a Caprese salad: some prefer just a drizzle of olive oil, others salt and pepper, and others balsamic vinegar. The author's current favorite is fresh ground pepper with a drizzle of balsamic glaze, which adds a slightly sweet and savory finish.
- Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

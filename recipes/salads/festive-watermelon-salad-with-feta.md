# Festive Watermelon Salad with Feta

## Description
A colorful, sweet-and-tart watermelon and feta salad that comes together in minutes — perfect for Memorial Day, July 4th, Labor Day, and other summer gatherings.

## Ingredients
_(for 10 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

- 1 seedless watermelon (7 to 8 pounds), chilled and cubed
- ½ cup extra virgin olive oil (or less)
- 3 limes, juiced
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup fresh chopped mint
- 1½ cups crumbled feta cheese (goat or sheep milk feta is best)

## Instructions
1. Combine all ingredients.
2. Toss!

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Try this recipe with honeydew melon, cantaloupe, or yellow watermelon.
- You could add some chopped cucumber to the mix too, for even more crunch.
- Store the watermelon salad in an airtight container in the fridge.

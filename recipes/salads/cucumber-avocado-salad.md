# High-Fiber Cucumber Avocado Salad

## Description
Crisp fresh cucumbers combined with creamy rich avocado pieces, drizzled in a tangy, flavorful homemade dressing — a simple chopped salad ready in 10 minutes. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/25348/High-Fiber-Cucumber-Avocado-Salad-Recipe-Is-the-Best-Side-Dish-to-Make-Today-10-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

Dressing
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar or apple cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons coconut aminos, tamari or Bragg's liquid aminos
- 2 tablespoons raw honey or pure maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (or to taste)

Salad
- 2 ripe, but firm, avocados, cut into bite-sized chunks
- 4 garden cucumbers, cut into bite-sized chunks
- 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup roasted cashews, coarsely chopped
- fresh lemon or lime wedges, for garnish

## Instructions
1. In a small jar, add all of the dressing ingredients. Shake vigorously to emulsify, or whisk the dressing ingredients together really well in a small bowl.
2. Put the vegetables into a salad bowl.
3. Drizzle the veggies with the dressing and gently toss to coat. Sprinkle with coarsely chopped cashews. Serve.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- This salad is best eaten right away, as avocados do turn brown quickly.
- Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge.
- Serve as a side dish with chicken, beef, pork, lamb or seafood, or as a light vegetarian lunch or dinner.

# Zesty Lemon Dill Tuna Salad

## Description
A mayo-based tuna salad developed by registered dietitian Dawn Menning, brightened with lemon zest and juice, fresh dill, shallot, celery, Greek yogurt, Dijon mustard, and a touch of sumac — a balanced, high-protein single-serving salad. [Source](https://www.fitandwell.com/recipes/high-protein-tuna-salad/)

## Ingredients
_(for 1 serving)_

- 5 oz can no-salt-added white tuna, drained and flaked
- 2 tbsp mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp finely chopped celery
- 2 tbsp finely chopped shallot
- 1 tbsp finely chopped fresh dill (plus extra for garnish)
- 1 tbsp whole-milk plain Greek yogurt
- 1 tsp grated lemon zest
- 1 tbsp lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1/4 tsp ground sumac
- 1/4 tsp sugar
- 1/8 tsp ground pepper
- Pinch of salt

_Optional, for serving (not included in nutrition info): wholewheat bread, celery sticks, or Bibb lettuce_

## Instructions
1. In a bowl, use a fork to mix together the tuna, mayonnaise, celery, shallot, dill, Greek yogurt, lemon zest, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, sumac, sugar, pepper, and salt. Mash and stir until the mixture is fully combined and creamy.
2. Transfer the salad to a plate and, if you like, top with a little extra fresh dill for garnish.
3. Serve as desired: spread on wholewheat bread, pair with celery sticks, or enjoy over a bed of Bibb lettuce.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Recipe developed by registered dietitian Dawn Menning (program director for digital health at the Nutu App and Willow Laboratories), who assimilated several tuna salad recipes shared with her over her career.
- Nutrition per serving (per source): 412 kcal, 25g fat (5g saturated), 8g carbohydrates, 4g sugar, 36g protein.

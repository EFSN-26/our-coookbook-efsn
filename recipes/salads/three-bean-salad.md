# Three Bean Salad

## Description
This three-bean salad is a wonderful addition to any meal. Rich in fiber, folate, iron and potassium, beans complement a heart-healthy diet and are also a great source of plant-based protein and iron for anyone following a vegetarian diet. Bring this colorful, healthy dish to a barbecue, serve it as an appetizer salad or side dish, and even pack it for lunch.

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings)_

- 1 1/2 pounds green beans, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1/4 cup white-wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
- 2/3 cup canned great northern beans, rinsed
- 2/3 cup canned pinto beans, rinsed
- 1 large red bell pepper, chopped
- 1 large carrot, peeled and chopped
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions
1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Prepare an ice bath in a large bowl. Add cut green beans to the boiling water and cook for 3 minutes. Use a slotted spoon to transfer the beans to the ice bath. Drain in a colander.
2. Whisk the olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice, chopped garlic, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Add the great northern beans, pinto beans, chopped bell pepper, chopped carrot, parsley, and the green beans; toss to coat in the dressing.

# Salada de grão-de-bico com cebola marinada
(Chickpea Salad with Marinated Onion)

## Description
This chickpea salad with marinated onion is a versatile, easy-to-prepare side dish. On the Brazilian TV show "É de Casa," host Thiago Oliveira demonstrated how to make this healthy and delicious salad. The key differentiator is the marinated onion, which becomes milder and tastier through the marinating process. Translated from the original Portuguese source.

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

**Salad**
- 1 Japanese/mini cucumber, chopped
- 1 tomato, seeded and chopped
- 1 cup cooked chickpeas
- Chopped parsley, to taste
- 2 tablespoons pitted green olives

**Marinated onion**
- 1/2 red onion, sliced
- Juice of 1 lemon (Sicilian/regular lemon)
- Salt, to taste
- 1 teaspoon hot paprika
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

**Dressing**
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt, to taste
- 1 teaspoon oregano

## Instructions
1. **Salad:** Mix together the cucumber, tomato, cooked chickpeas, chopped parsley, and green olives.
2. **Marinated onion:** Mix all the marinated onion ingredients together. Let it marinate while you prepare the rest of the recipe.
3. **Dressing:** Mix all the dressing ingredients together and pour the dressing over the salad.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
The source notes the marinated onion is the standout element of this dish, becoming softer and more flavorful through marinating alongside the paprika, lemon juice, and olive oil.

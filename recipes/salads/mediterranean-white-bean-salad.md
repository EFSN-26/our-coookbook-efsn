# Creamy Mediterranean White Bean Salad

## Description
"Super easy, bright, fresh side dish for those backyard parties and barbecues" — this creamy white bean salad with fresh chopped vegetables gets tastier a few hours later as the flavors meld, making it ideal for get-togethers and excellent next-day lunches.

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings)_

Salad:
- 30 ounces (2 cans) white beans, rinsed well and drained
- 8 mini seedless cucumbers, sliced
- 1 small red onion, finely diced
- 2 cups ripe cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 yellow bell pepper, seeded and diced
- 1/2 cup pitted olives, halved
- 1 bunch fresh parsley, chopped (about 1/4 to 1/3 cup), or to taste
- 1/2 bunch fresh dill, chopped (about 1/4 cup), or to taste
- 1/3 cup freshly shaved Parmesan cheese

Dressing:
- 1/4 cup mayonnaise (homemade or good-quality store-bought)
- 2 large garlic cloves, pressed
- zest and juice of 1 large lemon
- 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar, red wine vinegar, or apple cider vinegar

## Instructions
1. Put the white beans into a large mixing bowl. Add the chopped vegetables and herbs. Stir in the Parmesan cheese. (Use whatever type of white beans you like — cannellini and great northern are good choices.)
2. To make the dressing, put the ingredients into a small jar and shake to emulsify. (You could also whisk together in a bowl.) Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Season with salt and black pepper, if desired.
3. Refrigerate the salad for at least 15 minutes to allow the flavors to combine. Toss again and serve.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- This salad can easily be doubled or tripled for larger families or get-togethers.
- Best enjoyed the day of making, but stays good in an airtight container in the refrigerator for two days.
- Making it hours in advance lets you relax and enjoy your company, and it also makes for an excellent next-day lunch.

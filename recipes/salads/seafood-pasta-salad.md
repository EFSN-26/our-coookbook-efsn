# Seafood Pasta Salad

## Description
A creamy pasta salad loaded with crab meat, shrimp, peas and celery, all coated in a light and tangy mayonnaise-sour cream dressing — great served chilled or slightly warmed for a potluck, picnic or family cookout. [Source](https://xoxobella.com/seafood-pasta-salad/)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings — see §8 for scaling; total time includes 1 hour chilling)_

- 1 Pound shell pasta, medium size, cooked, drained, and cooled
- 8 Ounces crab meat, imitation or lump crab
- 8 Ounces shrimp, frozen cooked, tails removed and thawed
- 1 Cup peas, thawed
- 1 Cup celery, minced
- ½ Cup mayonnaise
- ½ Cup sour cream
- 2 Tablespoons white vinegar
- 1 Tablespoon granulated sugar
- 1 Teaspoon salt
- ½ Teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions
1. In a large bowl, combine the pasta, crab meat, salad shrimp, peas and celery. Set aside.
2. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, sour cream, white vinegar, sugar, salt and pepper.
3. Pour over the pasta, seafood and veggies and toss until the dressing is well distributed.
4. Chill for 1 hour before serving.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Don't overcook the pasta — cook to "al dente" doneness or the salad will turn mushy.
- Drain and dry the shrimp thoroughly; frozen shrimp tends to retain a lot of water.
- Mix the dressing separately before combining — whisking it on its own gives a smoother result.
- Customize ratios of seafood and veggies to taste; the recipe is very forgiving.
- Storage: refrigerate in an airtight container for up to 3-4 days. Not recommended for freezing — it becomes watery when thawed.

# Peach Blueberry Cucumber Salad with Feta in Lemon Basil Vinaigrette

## Description
A no-cook summer salad combining sweet peaches, sweet-tart blueberries, cool mini cucumbers, and salty feta, tossed in a lemon basil vinaigrette. Works as a light lunch or a side for grilled dinners such as grilled chicken. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/103072/Peach-Blueberry-Cucumber-Salad-Recipe-With-Feta-In-a-Refreshing-Lemon-Basil-Vinaigrette-15-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 3 ripe peaches, pitted and chopped
- 2 cups fresh blueberries
- 4 mini cucumbers, sliced
- 1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
- 2 small lemons, juice and zest
- 1 1/2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1 1/2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 1/2 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons basil, chopped
- salt and pepper, to taste (optional; source used about 1/3 teaspoon each)

## Instructions
1. In a large mixing bowl, add the peaches, blueberries, and cucumbers. Stir to combine.
2. Add the feta cheese and toss gently to combine.
3. In a medium mixing bowl, add the lemon juice and zest, mustard, olive oil, and honey. Season with salt and pepper to taste, if desired. Whisk to combine.
4. Add the basil and stir to combine. Taste and adjust dressing ingredients as needed.
5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to combine.
6. Transfer to a serving bowl (or individual bowls). Garnish with additional basil if desired, and serve. Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Any stone fruit can be swapped for the peaches if unavailable (e.g., nectarines or plums) — per source.
- The source notes cook times, nutrition, and servings are approximate and can vary with ingredient and equipment differences.

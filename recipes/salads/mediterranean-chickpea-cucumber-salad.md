# Mediterranean Chickpea Cucumber Salad

## Description
A light yet filling vegetarian salad built around chickpeas and cucumber, wrapped in a creamy, tangy Greek yogurt dressing brightened with fresh dill, parsley and chives. A great addition to a Mediterranean-diet rotation, and a great option for lunch, a light dinner, or a picnic side dish.

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

### For the Dressing
- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice (the juice of 1 small lemon)
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon honey
- kosher salt and black pepper, to taste (about 1 teaspoon of each)
- 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped (plus more for serving)
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped (plus more for serving)

### For the Salad
- 45 ounces (3 cans) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 6 mini cucumbers, diced to about the same size as the chickpeas
- 1/3 cup fresh chives, chopped

## Instructions
1. In a small mixing bowl, add the Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, lemon juice, Dijon mustard and honey. Season with salt and pepper, to taste. Whisk to combine, and taste to see if you'd like to add more of any ingredient.
2. Add the fresh dill and parsley. Stir to combine, then set aside.
3. In a large mixing bowl, add the chickpeas. (Before using, pour the chickpeas into a colander and run under cold water to drain and rinse.)
4. Add the cucumbers and chives. Toss to combine.
5. Scoop the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. If you have 30 minutes to spare, let the salad marinate in the dressing at room temperature to allow the flavors to meld. Garnish with additional fresh herbs and serve.
6. Store leftover salad in an airtight container in the refrigerator. Allow it to sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes before serving again.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Letting the assembled salad marinate about 30 minutes before serving (beyond the 15-minute active prep) allows the flavors to meld, per the source.
- Leftovers keep in an airtight container in the fridge; let refrigerated leftovers sit at room temperature for about 30 minutes before re-serving.

# Creamy Amish Cucumber Salad

## Description
A simple, creamy Amish-style cucumber salad — light and refreshing, and a perfect side dish with beef, chicken, pork or seafood.

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 2 cucumbers, thinly sliced (peeled, if desired)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 onion (white, red or yellow), sliced
- 2 tablespoons vinegar
- sour cream (2 tablespoons to 1/4 cup)

## Instructions
1. Put the cucumber slices into a bowl. Sprinkle them with the salt. Let them sit about 5 minutes and then pat with paper towels to remove moisture.
2. Add the onions. Drizzle with the vinegar. Add the sour cream until desired creaminess is achieved. Season with pepper, if desired.
3. Chill until ready to serve. Store the cucumber salad in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to three days.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
For a little spice, add some crushed red pepper flakes or a pinch of cayenne. For even more flavor, use seasoned salt. Not a fan of sour cream? Use light mayonnaise or Greek yogurt instead. A cucumber is about 95 percent water with roughly 16 calories per cucumber, making this a light, low-calorie side dish.

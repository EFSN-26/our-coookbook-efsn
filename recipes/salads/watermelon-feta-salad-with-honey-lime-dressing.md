# Watermelon Feta Salad with Honey Lime Dressing

## Description
Sweet watermelon pairs with salty feta cheese, crunchy cucumber and onion, and fresh mint, all tied together with a honey lime dressing. A cool, refreshing salad perfect for hot summer days.

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- ¼ watermelon, cubed (enough for 3 to 4 cups of cubes)
- 1 mini cucumber, thinly sliced
- ¼ red onion, sliced thin
- ¼ cup feta cheese, crumbled
- 1 tablespoon fresh mint, chopped
- ¼ cup honey lime dressing (see Cook's Notes)

## Instructions
1. Cut the watermelon into bite-sized cubes. Place in a medium to large bowl.
2. Thinly slice the red onion and cucumber and add to the bowl.
3. Add the feta cheese and chopped mint.
4. Drizzle the salad with the honey lime dressing and toss gently to mix.
5. Salad is ready to serve right away.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Honey lime dressing (from the source's linked recipe): whisk together ¼ cup Dijon mustard, ¼ cup honey, 2 tablespoons sugar, 2 tablespoons grapeseed or sesame oil, 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar, and 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice; blend on high for 30-60 seconds until smooth. Makes about 1 cup (serving size about 2 tablespoons).
- Serve alongside any grilled food, such as BBQ chicken, grilled pork tenderloin, or a stuffed burger.
- The watermelon starts releasing water as soon as it's mixed with salty feta, so leftovers won't keep long — best enjoyed the same day, though it can be refrigerated overnight in a pinch.
- Nutrition estimate for the salad alone (not including dressing): 117 kcal per serving.

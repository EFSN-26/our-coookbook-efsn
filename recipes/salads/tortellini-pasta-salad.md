# Tortellini Pasta Salad

## Description
Cheese-stuffed tortellini tossed with cherry tomatoes, mozzarella, red onion, and pesto — a hearty pasta salad that's substantial enough to be a main course, and easy to make ahead for a picnic or cookout.

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

- 20 ounces tortellini
- 12 ounces mozzarella
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes
- ⅛ cup red onion or shallot
- 1 cup pesto
- 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
- Salt & pepper, to taste
- Basil, to garnish

## Instructions
1. Cook tortellini in salted water as directed on the box.
2. Drain the tortellini and rinse with cold water.
3. Put them in a bowl and add the mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, red onion, pesto, and red wine vinegar. Mix well.
4. Taste and add salt and pepper if needed. Garnish with basil.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Don't overcook the tortellini — follow the package directions and cook only until al dente.
- Try cubed provolone or smoked gouda instead of mozzarella for a different twist.
- Not a fan of tomatoes or onions? Swap in diced bell peppers, cucumbers, or artichoke hearts.
- If you're not into pesto, a good Italian dressing works well too.
- Great to make ahead — just wait to add the basil garnish until right before serving.
- Cover and refrigerate; it will stay fresh for up to 5 days. Stir before serving to redistribute the pesto, and add a drizzle of olive oil or extra pesto if it seems dry.

# Mexican Street Corn-Inspired Bean Salad

## Description
This Mexican street corn-inspired bean salad is creamy, with lime and mild chili seasoning, made hearty with black beans. It's a novel bean salad with elote flavors, a snap to put together.

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 1/4 cup lime juice
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/4 teaspoon chili-lime seasoning or chili powder
- 30 ounces cans black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 (14.75 ounce) can Mexican-style street corn, drained
- 1/2 cup chopped red onion
- 1 fresh jalapeno, chopped (seeded, if desired), plus more for garnish
- 1/2 cup crumbled cotija cheese
- 1/3 cup chopped cilantro, plus more for garnish

## Instructions
1. Stir together mayonnaise, lime juice, garlic powder, cumin, and chili-lime seasoning in a large bowl until smooth.
2. Add black beans, corn, red onion, jalapeño, cotija cheese, and 1/3 cup cilantro. Gently stir until well coated.
3. Garnish with additional chopped jalapeños and cilantro. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate up to 3 days before serving.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
Recipe developed by Jaelyn Luong. Nutrition per serving (per source): 476 calories, 32g fat, 38g carbs, 13g protein. Can be made ahead and refrigerated, covered, for up to 3 days before serving.

# Amish Cabbage Soup Recipe With Ground Beef

## Description
An Amish-style cabbage soup loaded with tender cabbage, carrots, potatoes and ground beef in a tomato-juice broth — a hearty, high-fiber, high-protein soup that was made long before cabbage soup became trendy for weight loss. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/31019/Amish-Cabbage-Soup-Recipe-With-Ground-Beef-High-Fiber-High-Protein)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 onion, chopped
- 3/4 cup celery, chopped
- 2 cups carrots, chopped
- 2 cups potatoes, diced
- 1 head cabbage, sliced
- 2 cups tomato juice
- 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
- 2 teaspoons salt, or to taste
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, or to taste
- 2 cups hot water

## Instructions
1. Brown the ground beef with the onion in a large soup pot.
2. Add the remaining ingredients (other vegetables like broccoli, cauliflower or parsnips can also be added). Simmer gently for about 1 hour.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- This soup freezes well — make extra for later.
- Store leftovers in an airtight container and reheat in the microwave.
- Serve with your favorite side dishes, like a chunk of bread or a side salad.

# Savory Rotisserie Chicken Broth

## Description
A budget-friendly way to turn the leftover bones, wings and skin from a rotisserie chicken into a rich, savory homemade broth, simmered with carrots, celery, onion and garlic. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/2370/Savory-Rotisserie-Chicken-Broth-Recipe-Save-the-Scraps-From-That-Costco-Chicken-5-Ingredients)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- leftover rotisserie chicken bones, wings and skin (quantity not specified by source)
- 2 carrots
- 1 celery stalk
- 1 small onion, peeled
- 2 garlic cloves, peeled

## Instructions
1. Add the leftover rotisserie chicken (bones, wings and skin) to a stock pot with the carrots, celery, onion and garlic cloves. (Fresh herbs can be added while simmering for extra flavor.)
2. Pour in enough water to cover.
3. Bring the broth to a boil and then simmer for at least 2 hours.
4. Strain the liquid for a chicken broth ready to use in any recipe calling for broth. Store in airtight containers or mason jars in the fridge, or freeze to use later.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Add fresh herbs to the stock while simmering for extra flavor.

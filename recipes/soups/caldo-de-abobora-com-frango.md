# Caldo de Abóbora com Frango (Pumpkin and Chicken Soup)

## Description
A simple, easy Brazilian pumpkin-and-chicken soup made for cold days — quick to prepare with few ingredients, with ginger giving it a special kick. This dish can be frozen for days when you don't want to cook.

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 chopped onion
- 1 tablespoon grated ginger
- 1 whole, skinless chicken breast
- 2 peeled tomatoes
- 500 grams peeled pumpkin, cut into cubes
- Salt, to taste
- 2 liters hot water
- Chopped parsley, to taste

## Instructions
1. In a pot, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil and sauté the chopped onion and grated ginger for 4 minutes.
2. Add the chicken breast and peeled tomatoes. Cover the pot and let it cook for 5 minutes.
3. Turn the chicken over and let it cook for another 5 minutes.
4. Add the cubed pumpkin, salt to taste, and the 2 liters of hot water. Cover the pot and cook for 40 minutes.
5. Remove the chicken, shred it, and set it aside.
6. Transfer the soup from the pot to a blender and blend until smooth.
7. Return the soup to the pot, add the reserved shredded chicken, and bring to a boil.
8. Finish with chopped parsley to taste and serve immediately.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- This soup can be frozen for the days you don't feel like cooking.

# Easy Aztec Black Bean Soup

## Description
A quick, gluten-free chipotle black bean tortilla soup built on sautéed onion and red bell pepper, chipotle salsa, chicken broth, black beans, and corn — ready in about 30 minutes. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/36959/Easy-Aztec-Black-Bean-Soup-Recipe-Is-What-to-Make-This-Weekend-30-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 onion, diced
- 1/3 cup chopped red bell pepper
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
- 1 1/2 cups chipotle salsa
- 4 cups chicken broth
- 30 ounces (2 cans) black beans, drained and rinsed
- 15 ounces (1 can) whole kernel corn, drained

## Instructions
1. Heat the oil in a large soup pot. Add the onion and red bell pepper. Season with salt and pepper. Cook until soft, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook 30 seconds. Add the cumin and chili powder. Cook, stirring, about 30 seconds.
2. Add the salsa and broth. Bring to a simmer, then add the black beans and corn. Reduce heat, cover and simmer, stirring occasionally, about 20 minutes. Season with more salt and pepper, if needed.
3. Ladle into bowls and top with desired garnishes.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Optional additions: chopped cooked chicken or cooked ground beef can be added in step 1.
- If you can't find chipotle salsa, use your favorite salsa instead.
- Suggested toppings: sour cream, lime wedges, fresh chopped cilantro, chopped red onion, tortilla chips, chopped avocado, sliced jalapeños.
- Storage: keep in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 4 days.
- The recipe uses chicken broth, so it is not vegetarian as written; substitute vegetable broth for a vegetarian version.

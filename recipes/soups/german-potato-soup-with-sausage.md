# German Potato Soup with Sausage

## Description
A hearty, stick-to-your-ribs soup that's simple to make and full of flavor — potatoes, carrots, celery, and onions paired with smoky ring sausage in every bite.

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 ribs celery, sliced
- 1 cup sliced carrots
- 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon dried parsley
- 2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 14 ounces ring smoked sausage, sliced
- 64 ounces chicken broth
- salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Melt butter in a large soup pot over medium heat. Add onion, celery, carrots, thyme, and parsley. Cook 5 minutes.
2. Add potatoes, sausage, and broth. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer 25 minutes.
3. Ladle into bowls. Season with salt and pepper. For a thicker consistency, smash some of the potatoes with the ladle before serving.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
For a richer base, some cooks purée a couple cups of the cooked soup with an immersion blender and stir it back in.

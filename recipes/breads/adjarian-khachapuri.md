# Easy Georgian Cheese Bread (Adjarian Khachapuri)

## Description
Adjarian khachapuri is a boat-shaped cheese bread that originated in Georgia's Adjara region, known for being super cheesy with a sunny-side up egg or egg yolk on top. This easy version is simple to make at home. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/20900/Easy-Georgian-Cheese-Bread-Is-High-Protein-Deliciousness-Adjarian-Khachapuri-9-Ingredients)

## Ingredients
_(for 12 servings)_

Dough
- 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
- 1 cup milk, warmed
- 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoons canola oil
- 3 cups flour

Topping
- 5 cups shredded mozzarella (you could also use a mixture of mozzarella and crumbled feta)
- 1 tablespoon butter, cut into small pieces
- 1 whole egg or egg yolk

Egg Wash
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 tablespoon water

## Instructions
1. To make the dough, combine the warm milk, sugar, salt and yeast in a bowl. Stir. Let the mixture sit for about 5 minutes or until it begins to froth.
2. Stir in the oil and egg.
3. Slowly start adding the flour until it forms a dough (you may not need all the flour). Knead the dough a few times until it comes together into a smooth dough. Put the dough into an oiled bowl and cover with a kitchen towel. Let the dough rise for about 30 minutes or until it doubles in size.
4. Flour a work surface. Divide the dough into five pieces. Roll each piece of dough into an oblong shape about 8 inches by 12 inches. Put the dough onto a baking sheet.
5. Sprinkle each piece of dough with 1 cup of the cheese. Fold the edges in and pinch the ends together to form the boat shape.
6. Combine the egg yolk and water to create an egg wash. Brush the sides of the dough with the egg wash.
7. Dot the top of the cheese with the butter pieces.
8. Bake in a preheated 425-degree F oven for about 20 to 30 minutes, or until bread is golden. About 10 minutes before it's done baking, crack an egg on top (or just use the yolk) and continue cooking.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- You could also use a mixture of mozzarella and crumbled feta for the topping.

# Nonna's No-Knead Pizza Dough

## Description
An easy no-knead pizza dough — the author's nonna frequently skipped kneading and still got a tender crust. Makes enough dough for two 10- to 12-inch pizzas. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/54956/Nonnas-No-Knead-Pizza-Dough-Recipe-for-Pizza-Night-6-Ingredients-30-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 16 servings; makes two 10- to 12-inch pizzas)_

- 1 cup warm water (about 105 degrees F)
- .25 ounces (1 packet) active dry yeast
- 2 teaspoons granulated sugar
- 2 cups all-purpose flour (or gluten-free flour)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions
1. Whisk together the water, sugar and yeast. Let the mixture sit for 5 minutes — it will start to foam.
2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the flour and salt. Add the yeast mixture and the olive oil. Mix until well combined. Cover the bowl with a towel and let it rise for about 20 minutes. (For a gluten-free crust, use gluten-free 1:1 flour.)
3. Remove the dough with floured hands and roll into two balls. The dough is ready to be shaped and baked. This recipe can easily be doubled for four pizzas.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- To bake: put the rolled-out dough onto a baking sheet, pizza stone or pizza pan sprinkled with cornmeal. Prick holes all over the crust with a fork, add desired sauce and toppings, then bake in a preheated 450-degree F oven for about 10 to 12 minutes.
- Use gluten-free 1:1 flour for a gluten-free crust.
- The recipe can easily be doubled to make four pizzas.

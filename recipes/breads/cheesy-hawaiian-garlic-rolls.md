# Cheesy Hawaiian Garlic Rolls

## Description
Fluffy King's Hawaiian Savory Butter Rolls stuffed with mozzarella string cheese and brushed with a garlic-parmesan butter, then topped with more mozzarella and baked until melty and golden. A simple, crowd-pleasing bread for almost any meal.

## Ingredients
_(for 9 servings)_

- 1 package King's Hawaiian Savory Butter Rolls (12 rolls)
- mozzarella string cheese (you'll need one stick, per two rolls)
- 1 stick of butter, melted
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- 3 tablespoons shredded parmesan
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F. Place a package of rolls into a sprayed baking dish. Cut two slits in each roll, being careful not to cut through to the bottom. Cut mozzarella string cheese sticks into four pieces. Place a small piece of string cheese into each slit.
2. Mix together butter, minced garlic, parsley and parmesan and brush over rolls. Sprinkle the mozzarella over the top. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese is melted and a bit brown on top.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- The source used only 9 rolls (baked in a 9x9 pan) rather than all 12; the recipe works fine for all 12 rolls — just use a larger pan or baking sheet.
- This recipe will work fine with any type of precooked rolls; they don't have to be King's Hawaiian rolls specifically.
- Any spices and herbs your family enjoys can be swapped in; if using a different cheese, make sure it's a high-melting variety.

# Caipirinha

## Description
You can make Brazil's national cocktail at home with just four ingredients – cachaça, lime, sugar and ice. Warning – this tasty tippler packs a punch.

## Ingredients
_(for 1 serving)_

- 3 ounces cachaça
- ½ large lime
- 4 teaspoons granulated sugar
- ice cubes (cubes or crushed)

## Instructions
1. Wash lime and cut in half. Put one half aside and cut the remaining half into 4 wedges.
2. Place lime wedges and sugar into a low ball glass and muddle until thoroughly mixed.
3. Add cachaça and ice cubes.
4. Gently stir.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- You can adjust the sugar amount to modify the sweetness level.
- You can add extra ice cubes to dilute the Caipirinha.
- Should you run out of cachaça, you can use rum or vodka instead. In Brazil, the rum version is called a Caipirissima and the vodka version is called a Caipiroska.
- You can modify the recipe by replacing limes with other fruit like passion fruit, mango and pineapple (these fruity variations are called Caipafruta cocktails in Brazil).

# Vanilla Chai Protein Smoothie

## Description
A creamy, dessert-like smoothie that gets its big protein boost from Greek yogurt and cottage cheese rather than protein powder, flavored with warm Chai spices like cinnamon, cardamom, allspice, nutmeg, clove, and ginger.

## Ingredients
_(for 2 smoothies)_

- 1 frozen banana
- 1 cup cottage cheese
- ½ cup Greek yogurt
- ½ cup almond milk
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon cardamom
- 1 teaspoon allspice
- ½ teaspoon nutmeg
- ½ teaspoon ground clove
- ½ teaspoon ginger
- 1 tablespoon honey, or to taste

### Optional Ingredients
- 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
- 2 cinnamon sticks, for garnish
- 2 star anise, for garnish

## Instructions
1. Add all of the ingredients to a blender in the order listed.
2. Blend, using a tamper to push the ingredients towards the blades.
3. Serve immediately, garnishing each glass with a cinnamon stick and star anise, if desired.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Don't worry about the lumpy texture of cottage cheese — since everything is blended, it won't be noticeable.
- This recipe (2 servings) has about 38 grams of protein total, or 19 grams of protein per serving.
- To add about 3 grams more protein per serving, switch the almond milk to cow's milk or soy milk. With cow's milk, total protein amounts to 45 grams.
- For an even bigger protein boost while keeping the lower-calorie almond milk, add a scoop of vanilla protein powder, which can add up to 30 grams total (about 34 grams per serving).
- To make with chai tea instead of ground spices: steep a masala Chai tea bag in a cup of boiling water for several minutes, sweeten with honey or sugar to taste, then add ½ cup of the tea mixture to the smoothie ingredients and blend. If the mixture becomes too thin, add another half frozen banana or a few more scoops of cottage cheese.

# Fresh Lemon Cucumber Mint Water

## Description
A crisp, naturally refreshing infused water made by steeping lemon (or lime), cucumber and fresh mint leaves in ice water. It's an easy way to make plain water more interesting and stay hydrated through the summer. [Source](https://www.masalaherb.com/youll-want-a-pitcher-of-this-fresh-lemon-cucumber-mint-water-all-summer/)

## Ingredients
_(for servings not specified by source — makes 1 pitcher/jar)_

- lemon or lime, organic, sliced
- cucumber, sliced lengthwise into thin slices
- mint leaves, fresh, washed
- water
- ice cubes

## Instructions
1. Keep your glass, pitcher or bottle ready.
2. Slice your clean lemon/lime.
3. Cut your clean cucumber lengthwise into thin slices.
4. Make sure your mint leaves are clean and washed.
5. Place as many lemon/lime slices, mint leaves and cucumber slices as you want into your glass/jar/bottle — the more you add, the more flavorful the water. Leave enough space to add water to infuse.
6. Add ice cubes and drinking water.
7. Store in a cool place. Enjoy the first round after about 30 minutes.
8. Refill again up to 4 times max with fresh cool water, letting the ingredients infuse each time before enjoying again.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
Use organic lemon/lime because the whole fruit infuses the water (avoid pesticides); the same goes for the mint and cucumber. Use mineral-enhanced water for added nutrition. Use a glass or jar that can be closed (such as a milk-bottle-style jar) for hygienic reasons, or a pitcher/portable fruit infuser bottle for taking it on the go.

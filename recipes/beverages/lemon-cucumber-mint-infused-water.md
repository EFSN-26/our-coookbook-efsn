# Lemon Cucumber Mint Infused Water

## Description
This simple infused water has all the calming vibes of a spa day without the appointment. The mix of lemon, cucumber, and mint feels clean, fresh, and instantly soothing — an easy way to make daily hydration feel a little more luxurious. [Source](https://thehonoursystem.com/lemon-cucumber-mint-infused-water-9/)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 2 lemons, thinly sliced
- 1/2 large cucumber, thinly sliced
- 20 mint leaves
- 2 liters fresh filtered water

## Instructions
1. Split the lemon slices, mint leaves, and cucumber slices between 2 large 32-ounce mason jars.
2. Top with fresh, filtered water and screw on the lids. Store overnight in the fridge to allow the water to infuse.
3. Drink from the jars on the go or pour into a glass.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Re-use the lemons, mint, and cucumber left in the jars: once the water is drunk, top the jars up with more fresh water and leave overnight again in the fridge for a second use.
- Switch up the citrus with limes, oranges, or grapefruit.
- Add fruit flavor with fresh strawberries, blueberries, kiwi, mango, raspberries, blackberries, pears, or watermelon.
- Swap in different herbs like basil or rosemary.
- Add a few slices of fresh ginger for additional anti-inflammatory benefit.

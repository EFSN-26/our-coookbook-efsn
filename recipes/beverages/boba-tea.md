# Boba Tea (Basic Recipe)

## Description
Get the ultimate bubble tea experience at home with this easy-to-follow basic boba recipe: brew delicious tea, cook chewy tapioca pearls, and assemble a refreshing, customizable bubble tea treat.

## Ingredients
_(for 2 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

- 2 cups water
- 2 teabags black, green, or oolong tea
- 1/2 cup tapioca pearls (boba)
- 1/4 cup honey, sugar, or syrup
- 1 cup milk or cream
- Ice cubes

## Instructions
1. In a saucepan, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Remove from heat and add the teabags. Let steep for 5 minutes, then remove the teabags and allow the tea to cool.
2. In another saucepan, cook the tapioca pearls (boba) according to package instructions, usually by boiling them for 10-15 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
3. In a separate container, mix the cooked boba with the honey, sugar, or syrup.
4. In two glasses or cups, divide the brewed tea and add 1/2 cup of milk or cream to each.
5. Add the boba mixture to the tea and milk mixture.
6. Stir gently to distribute the boba, then add ice cubes to each cup.
7. Serve immediately with a wide straw to enjoy the chewy boba pearls.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Frozen tapioca pearls stay good for much longer than a home-cooked batch, so they're a convenient option.
- Play with different proportions of tea and milk to suit your taste, and experiment with add-ins like fruit or flavored syrups.
- Popping boba (made via a spherification process, bursting with fruit juice) is a different type of pearl from tapioca boba and can be used as an alternative or addition.

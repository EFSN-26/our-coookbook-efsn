# Whipped Strawberry Lemonade

## Description
This creamy whipped strawberry lemonade gets its frothy texture from blended coconut milk and ice, while fresh strawberries add bright berry flavor and a naturally pink hue. A quick homemade lemon syrup gives the drink balanced sweetness and plenty of citrus flavor, making it a refreshing warm-weather sip.

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 1/3 cup water
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 2 teaspoons grated lemon zest
- 2½ cups ice cubes
- 1 cup canned coconut milk, well stirred
- 1/2 cup lemon juice
- 1 cup chopped strawberries, plus sliced strawberries for garnish

## Instructions
1. Combine 1/3 cup water, 1/4 cup sugar and 2 teaspoons lemon zest in a small saucepan. Bring to a boil over high heat. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the sugar dissolves and the mixture turns yellow, about 3 minutes. Remove from heat. Cover and let steep for 1 hour. Strain through a fine-mesh strainer; discard the solids.
2. Combine 2½ cups ice, 1 cup coconut milk, the lemon simple syrup and 1/2 cup lemon juice in a blender. Blend until the ice is crushed and the mixture is slushy, about 30 seconds. Add 1 cup strawberries; blend until smooth, about 30 seconds more. Divide among 4 glasses. Garnish with sliced strawberries, if desired.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
Use only full-fat coconut milk — its higher fat content lets the mixture whip up properly. Choose strawberries that are bright red and fragrant for the best sweetness and aroma. Blueberries and limes can be substituted for a twist on the classic combo. Frozen strawberries can be used instead of fresh (may need less ice). Leftover lemon simple syrup is versatile — use it to sweeten iced tea, drizzle over pancakes/waffles, add to fruit salads or baked goods, glaze meats/vegetables, or freeze into ice cubes. Make ahead: the lemon simple syrup (step 1) can be refrigerated in an airtight container up to 3 days. Best enjoyed fresh the day it's made; leftovers keep in a covered container in the fridge about 5 days. Nutrition per serving (per source): 166 calories, 12g fat, 16g carbs, 1g protein.

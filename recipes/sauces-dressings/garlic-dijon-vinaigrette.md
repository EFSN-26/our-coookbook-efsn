# Garlic-Dijon Vinaigrette

## Description
This garlicky mustard vinaigrette is great to have in your repertoire, as it goes with almost anything. The recipe makes plenty, so you can fix it once and then have it on hand for several days. The garlic is delicious but bold here; use fewer cloves if you want to tone it down.

## Ingredients
_(for 16 servings)_

- 1/2 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1/4 cup lemon juice
- 1/4 cup red-wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 4 small cloves garlic, minced
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- Freshly ground pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Combine the oil, lemon juice, vinegar, mustard, minced garlic, salt, and freshly ground pepper to taste in a jar with a tight-fitting lid.
2. Cover and shake until emulsified.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
To make ahead: cover and refrigerate for up to 1 week. The garlic is bold here; use fewer cloves if you want to tone it down.

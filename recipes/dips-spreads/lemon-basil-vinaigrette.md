# Mediterranean Lemon Basil Vinaigrette

## Description
A six-ingredient Mediterranean vinaigrette inspired by fresh-grown basil, combining lemon juice, Dijon mustard, honey and green onion. Works as a salad dressing, a sauce for proteins/vegetables/grains, or a marinade. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/88800/Mediterranean-Lemon-Basil-Vinaigrette-Salad-Dressing-Recipe-6-Ingredients-10-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings)_

- 2/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 1/3 cup lemon juice (the juice from 2 lemons; pulp included if desired)
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon honey
- 1 green onion (scallion), finely diced
- 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
- salt and black pepper, to taste (optional; source used about 1/3 teaspoon of each)

## Instructions
1. In a small mixing bowl, add the olive oil, lemon juice, mustard and honey. Season with salt and pepper, to taste, if desired. Whisk to blend. (Fresh-squeezed lemon juice is preferred, but bottled lemon juice works in a pinch.)
2. Add the green onion and basil leaves and whisk until the dressing is emulsified.
3. If using as a salad dressing, serve over salad immediately. If using as a sauce, pour over any protein, vegetable or grain and serve. If using as a marinade, pour over protein of choice and marinate for at least an hour. If the sauce separates before serving, simply whisk to reincorporate.
4. Store leftover dressing in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Also works well as a sauce (the source served it over chicken breast) or as a marinade.
- Use fresh-squeezed lemon juice for best flavor.

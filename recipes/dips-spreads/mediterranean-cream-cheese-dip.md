# Mediterranean Cream Cheese Dip

## Description
An easy Mediterranean cream cheese dip served as a cream cheese board — a delicious snack perfect for one or two people, or easily doubled or tripled to serve a crowd.

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 8 oz. plain cream cheese (dairy or non-dairy)
- 1 tbsp. extra virgin olive oil
- 1/4 cup chopped, sun-dried tomatoes
- 2 tbsp. chopped Kalamata olives
- 2 tbsp. fresh, chopped Italian parsley
- 3 tbsp. Feta cheese crumbles
- 1/4 cup finely chopped cucumber
- 1/3 cup halved grape tomatoes
- 2 tsp. balsamic vinegar (or fresh lemon juice)

## Instructions
1. Spread the cream cheese over a charcuterie board.
2. Spread the chopped cucumber over the cream cheese.
3. Add the chopped sun-dried tomatoes.
4. Sprinkle on the Feta cheese crumbles.
5. Sprinkle the chopped parsley over the top.
6. Drizzle the olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the top. Use as much or as little as you like.
7. Add crackers around the edge or put them on a separate plate and serve.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
Use a regular, firm cream cheese (not whipped) — it goes straight from the fridge onto the board without softening first. This isn't really a make-ahead dish; the cream cheese and sun-dried tomatoes can be spread ahead, but everything else should be added fresh right before serving. Serve cold or at room temperature. Store leftovers in the fridge, covered with plastic wrap, for up to three days; freezing is not recommended. Vegan: use vegan cream cheese and vegan feta (or omit the feta). Keto: use full-fat cream cheese and cut the sun-dried tomatoes in half. Try tortilla chips, pita chips, crackers, breadsticks, crostini, cheese crisps, or raw veggie sticks for dipping.

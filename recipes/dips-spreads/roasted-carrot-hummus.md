# Roasted Carrot Hummus

## Description
Roasted carrots deepen into a caramelized sweetness that blends with chickpeas and tahini into a smooth, golden hummus with a hint of smoked paprika.

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

- 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 1 pound carrots, peeled and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 cup tahini
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, plus more for drizzling
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
- salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 204°C (400°F). Toss the carrot pieces with 1 tablespoon of olive oil, then season with salt and black pepper. Spread on a baking sheet and roast 20-25 minutes, until tender and lightly caramelized.
2. In a food processor, combine the roasted carrots, chickpeas, garlic, tahini, the remaining 2 tablespoons olive oil, lemon juice, cumin, and smoked paprika. Process until smooth and creamy, scraping down the sides as needed.
3. Taste and season with additional salt and black pepper as needed. If too thick, add a splash of water or more olive oil and blend again.
4. Transfer to a serving bowl. Optionally drizzle with more olive oil and sprinkle with smoked paprika. Serve with pita bread, vegetables, or crackers.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Roast the carrots until fully golden and tender for the best depth of flavor - don't rush this step.
- Blend longer than you think for the silkiest texture; add a little warm water or extra olive oil if it's too thick.
- Keeps in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 4 days; stir before serving as the olive oil can settle on top.

# Smooth Garlic Hummus (Israeli-Style)

## Description
A silky, from-scratch hummus made by simmering dried chickpeas with onion until they fall apart, then blending with tahini, lemon, and raw garlic. Cook time is long (about 2.5 hours) but mostly unattended.

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

- 1 cup chickpeas, soaked in water for 12 hours
- 1 whole onion, peeled
- 1/4 cup raw tahini
- juice of 1 lemon
- 4 garlic cloves, minced finely
- salt and pepper, to taste

### For serving:
- fruity extra virgin olive oil, to taste
- za'atar, to taste
- sumac, to taste

## Instructions
1. In a large pot, combine chickpeas and onion with enough water to cover, plus an additional 2 inches. Bring to a boil, cover, and cook on low heat until the chickpeas are completely soft and fall apart at the lightest touch, about 2-2.5 hours, until most of the water has evaporated. If chickpeas are slow to cook, increase heat to low-medium (no higher) - low and slow is key.
2. Once cooked, remove chickpeas from the liquid with a slotted spoon, reserving about 1/2 cup chickpeas for garnish. Reserve the cooking liquid.
3. In a food processor, combine the remaining chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and salt and pepper. Process until as smooth as possible - thin with water or reserved chickpea liquid, 1 tablespoon at a time, if needed. Taste and adjust seasoning.
4. Serve warm, drizzled with olive oil and topped with the reserved whole chickpeas, za'atar, and sumac. Eat with pita, crudites, or pieces of raw onion.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Tahini choice matters: the source recommends Dove-brand tahini, and cautions against Greek tahini, favoring Arabic tahini (in a white jar with an orange lid) as a good alternative.
- The onion is discarded after cooking - its purpose is only to help soften and flavor the chickpeas.
- Refrigerate in an airtight container for up to two weeks; freshly made hummus is generally best within two to three days. Can be frozen for up to three months (including in ice cube trays for small portions).

# Authentic Restaurant-Style Italian Bread Dipping Oil

## Description
A restaurant-copycat Italian herb and olive oil bread dip made from dried oregano, basil, parsley flakes, garlic powder, onion powder and crushed red pepper flakes, whisked into good-quality extra virgin olive oil. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/94388/Authentic-Restaurant-Style-Italian-Bread-Dipping-Oil-Recipe-5-Minutes-7-Ingredients)

## Ingredients
_(for 20 servings)_

- 3/4 teaspoon dried oregano
- 3/4 teaspoon parsley flakes
- 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
- 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
- 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes (or to taste)
- 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil

**Optional ingredients**
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese, freshly grated

## Instructions
1. Stir together the oregano, parsley flakes, dried basil, garlic powder, onion powder and crushed red pepper flakes. (If using the optional sea salt, add it now.)
2. Pour the olive oil into a small bowl. (Use a good-quality olive oil.)
3. Top with the seasoning blend and stir. Add the fresh garlic and Parmesan cheese, if using.
4. Serve with bread.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- This recipe can easily be cut in half or doubled.
- If you make extra seasoning blend, store it in an airtight container or plastic zipper bag in the pantry.
- Use a good-quality, extra-virgin olive oil — the source specifically calls this out as a non-negotiable for authentic flavor.
- Also good served with croutons, or topped with fresh olives.

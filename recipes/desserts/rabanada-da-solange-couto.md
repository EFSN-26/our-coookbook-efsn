# Rabanada da Solange Couto
(Solange Couto's Brazilian French Toast)

## Description
See how to make an easy rabanada (Brazilian French toast) with condensed milk, milk, and eggs. Actress Solange Couto taught host Felipe Titto how to prepare this dessert on the segment "Tá na Mesa, Pessoal!", a treat that is hugely popular during Brazilian Christmas and New Year's Eve celebrations. Translated from the original Portuguese source.

## Ingredients
_(for 10 servings)_

- 2 loaves of pão de rabanada (French-toast style bread), sliced not too thick
- 1 liter milk
- 1 can condensed milk
- 5 eggs
- 1 kilogram sugar
- 200 grams ground cinnamon
- 1 liter canola oil

## Instructions
1. In a large skillet, heat 1 liter of oil over high heat.
2. Blend 5 eggs, 1 can of condensed milk, and 1 liter of milk in a blender for 1 minute. Set aside.
3. On a tray or baking sheet, arrange the bread slices, cut 1.5 to 2 centimeters thick, side by side.
4. Pour the liquid mixture over the slices, turning each one to soak both sides evenly.
5. On another tray or baking sheet, mix the sugar and cinnamon. Set aside.
6. Fry the soaked bread slices in the hot oil, two at a time. Once golden, remove them from the skillet and place them directly onto the tray with the sugar-cinnamon mixture.
7. Arrange the fried rabanadas stacked so the heat forms a light syrup. Once cooled, restack the slices.
8. Pour the natural syrup that forms over the pyramid of rabanadas.

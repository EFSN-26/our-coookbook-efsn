# Bourbon Chocolate Derby Pie

## Description
An ooey, gooey chocolate and walnut derby pie spiked with bourbon. All the filling ingredients are mixed together and poured into an unbaked pie crust, then baked until the center is set. Serve with ice cream or whipped cream.

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings)_

- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup light corn syrup
- 3 tablespoons butter, melted
- 2 1/2 tablespoons bourbon
- dash of salt
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 3/4 cup mini semisweet chocolate chips
- 1 deep dish pie crust

## Instructions
1. In a bowl, combine the sugar, corn syrup, butter, bourbon, salt and eggs. Mix with an electric mixer until well combined.
2. Stir in the walnuts and chocolate chips. Pour into the unbaked pie crust.
3. Bake in a preheated 375-degree F oven for about 35 to 40 minutes, or until the center of the pie is set. Cool completely. Top with whipped cream before serving. Store the pie covered in the fridge for up to one week. Reheat in the microwave.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
As the pie bakes it separates and creates a golden layer on top — this is not a separate top crust. Serve with ice cream or whipped cream for an extra special treat.

# Date and Walnut Cake

## Description
A wholesome yet indulgent loaf cake, naturally sweetened with Medjool dates and packed with crunchy walnuts. It's wonderfully moist and rich, and works equally well as a comforting breakfast, an afternoon snack with tea, or a light dessert.

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings)_

- 15 pitted Medjool dates
- 1 cup warm milk
- 1 1/2 cups plain flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon bicarbonate of soda
- 2 eggs
- 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
- chocolate chips, optional

## Instructions
1. Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a loaf tin with parchment paper.
2. Place the pitted dates in a bowl with the warm milk and let them soak for 20 minutes until softened. Then blend until smooth.
3. Add the blended date mixture to a mixing bowl and stir in the flour, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, eggs, and walnuts. Mix until well combined.
4. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf tin, spreading it evenly.
5. Optionally, sprinkle extra walnuts and chocolate chips on top for added texture and flavor.
6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
7. Let the loaf cool before slicing and serving.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
Soaking the dates in warm milk before blending softens them for a smoother, moister batter. Chocolate chips are an optional add-in for extra indulgence.

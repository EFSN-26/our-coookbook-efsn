# Creamy Pineapple Sherbet

## Description
A refreshing, creamy frozen pineapple treat blended from frozen pineapple, sugar, heavy cream and lemon juice, then churned in an ice cream maker — pure sunshine in a scoop for hot summer days. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/26531/Creamy-Pineapple-Sherbet-Recipe-Is-Required-Summer-Enjoyment-5-Ingredients)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 2 cups pineapple chunks, frozen
- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup water
- 1/4 cup heavy cream
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice

## Instructions
1. Put the frozen pineapple, sugar and water into a blender. Blend until smooth.
2. Add the cream and lemon juice. Pulse to blend.
3. Pour into an ice cream maker and process according to manufacturer's directions.
4. Pour the processed sherbet into a container and freeze about 2 hours.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
Fresh-squeezed lemon juice is best, but bottled lemon juice works in a pinch. Store the sherbet in the freezer for up to three months.

# Banana Bread Crumb Cake

## Description
This utterly soft banana bread crumb cake is the perfect coffee cake recipe. A delightful blend of banana cake and crumb cake, this treat features moist and tender banana bread layers filled and topped with a buttery, irresistible streusel crumb. The brown sugar keeps this wonderful recipe soft and crave-worthy.

## Ingredients

**For the Crumb Topping:**

- 1/2 Cup salted butter, cold, (1 stick) cut into cubes
- 1 Cup brown sugar
- 1 Cup all purpose flour

**For the Cake:**

- 2 Cups all purpose flour
- 3 Teaspoons baking powder
- 1 Teaspoon salt
- 4 bananas, ripe, mashed, about 15 ounces if ripe, 11 ounces if frozen
- 1/2 Cup butter, at room temperature
- 2 Cups granulated sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 Teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 Cup whole milk

**Optional Powdered Sugar Glaze:**

- 1/2 Cup powdered sugar
- 2-4 Tablespoons heavy cream

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
2. In a small mixing bowl, combine the brown sugar and flour with a fork or pastry cutter, then add the cold cubed butter. Work this mixture until a sandy texture is achieved.
3. Coat a 9 x 13-inch baking pan with vegetable shortening or baking spray and set aside.
4. Pro tip: After greasing your pan, line it with a piece of parchment paper. This will allow you to easily lift the cake out to a cutting board for serving, once cooled.
5. In a medium bowl, mix the flour, baking powder and salt.
6. In a new large mixing bowl, stir together the mashed bananas, butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla, and mix until combined.
7. Note: There might be pieces of butter throughout this mixture which is okay – it will still bake well and completely in the oven.
8. Add half the flour and milk to the banana mixture, alternating, and mix to combine between each addition.
9. Pour the cake mix into the baking pan.
10. Top the cake mix with the crumb topping, evenly spreading the topping over the cake.
11. Bake for 50 to 55 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
12. When cooked, remove from the oven and allow to sit for 15 to 20 minutes on a cooling rack.
13. Serve warm or at room temperature, garnished with a powdered sugar glaze or whipped cream, if liked.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Use ripe bananas: The riper the bananas, the sweeter and more flavorful your banana coffee cake recipe will be.
- Room temperature ingredients: Bring butter, eggs, and milk to room temperature before mixing. This ensures even blending and a smoother batter.
- Don't overmix the cake batter: Small pieces of butter in the mixture are normal and will melt during baking, so avoid trying to get them overly blended and stirred through, otherwise you risk ending up with a dense cake.
- Prepare the pan properly: Grease your baking pan well with vegetable shortening or baking spray, and consider lining it with parchment paper for easy removal and cleanup.
- Even crumb topping: Distribute the crumb topping evenly over the batter to ensure a balanced texture throughout the cake.
- Check doneness: Use a cake tester or toothpick inserted into the center of the cake to check for doneness. It should come out clean or with a few moist crumbs, but not wet batter.

# Creamy Pumpkin Cheesecake Mousse

## Description
A no-bake, fluffy pumpkin cheesecake mousse that comes together in about 10 minutes with just six ingredients — fall dessert perfection without turning on the oven.

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 6 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup pumpkin puree
- 2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
- Cinnamon (optional)

## Instructions
1. Beat the whipped cream with an electric mixer until it gets frothy, about 2 to 3 minutes. Add the powdered sugar and vanilla. Continue to beat until soft peaks form. Set aside. (Make sure your cream cheese is at room temperature before starting the recipe.)
2. In another bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth. Add the pumpkin and pumpkin pie spice. Mix well. (Use canned pumpkin puree, not pumpkin pie filling.)
3. Gently fold in all but about 1 cup of the whipped cream into the pumpkin mixture.
4. Spoon or pipe the mousse into individual dessert dishes. Chill if desired. Serve with a dollop of remaining whipped cream and sprinkle with cinnamon. Store the mousse covered in the fridge.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Use canned pumpkin puree, not pumpkin pie filling.
- Make sure the cream cheese is at room temperature before starting.
- Store the mousse covered in the refrigerator.

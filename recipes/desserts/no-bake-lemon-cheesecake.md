# Matthew McConaughey's Favorite No-Bake Lemon Cheesecake

## Description
Camila Alves McConaughey's creamy no-bake lemon cheesecake — a homemade shortbread cookie crust with a sweet-tart, creamy filling of cream cheese, lemon juice, and sweetened condensed milk. No-bake, five ingredients, and easy to make.

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings — see §8 for scaling; plus prep/chill time — see Cook's Notes)_

- 1 cup crushed shortbread cookies
- 1/4 cup butter, melted
- 16 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 1/3 cup lemon juice (about 2 lemons)
- 1/2 cup sweetened condensed milk

Optional Ingredients:
- lemon slices and zest
- shredded coconut (Camila uses lemon slices)

## Instructions
1. Crush cookies, add melted butter and mix. Press into the bottom of a 6-inch springform pan. Refrigerate for 30 minutes. (To make this recipe gluten-free, use gluten-free cookies for the crust.)
2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese and lemon juice. Using a mixer, beat for 2 to 3 minutes or until well combined. (Fresh-squeezed lemon juice is always best, but you can use bottled in a pinch.)
3. Add the sweetened condensed milk and beat for another 2 to 3 minutes or until the cheesecake batter is light and fluffy. Spoon batter into the springform pan. Sprinkle a small amount of lemon zest on top of the cheesecake, if desired. Refrigerate at least six hours or overnight.
4. Remove the springform outer ring and place thinly sliced lemon slices around the edge of the cheesecake, or press shredded coconut around the edges, if desired. Slice and serve cold. Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Total prep time above (15 minutes) is active prep only — plan for an additional 30 minutes chilling the crust plus at least 6 hours (or overnight) chilling the filled cheesecake before serving.
- Can easily be modified to lime or orange instead of lemon.
- Recipe cooking times, nutritional information, and servings are approximate per the source.

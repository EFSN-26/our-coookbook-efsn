# Tyler Smith's French Bread Pizza

## Description
A nostalgic, simple French bread pizza topped with marinara, mozzarella, and pepperoni, finished with a drizzle of olive oil and hot honey. Recipe by Tyler Smith of Virginia Beach, Virginia, tested by the Taste of Home Test Kitchen.

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 1 loaf French bread
- 1.5 cups marinara or pizza sauce
- 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
- pepperoni, to taste
- Parmesan cheese, grated, to taste
- olive oil, for drizzling
- hot honey, to taste
- fresh basil, minced, to taste

## Instructions
1. Slice the French bread vertically in half (down the middle to make two sides), then slice horizontally to create a top and bottom.
2. Spread the sauce evenly onto the open side of the bread, then spread the cheese evenly on top of the sauce.
3. Top with whichever toppings you like (classic pepperoni and cheese, or your choice).
4. Bake at 375°F for 10-12 minutes, then broil for 2-3 minutes to get some color on the cheese (watch closely).
5. Remove from the oven and let cool for 5 minutes before serving.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
The recipe's simplicity is part of its appeal. Toppings are flexible — the author kept it classic with pepperoni and cheese.

# Crunchy Ranch Pan-fried Tilapia Recipe

## Description
A budget-friendly, high-protein seafood dinner: tilapia fillets coated in a simple breadcrumb-and-dry-ranch mixture, then quickly pan-fried in olive oil until golden brown and crispy. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/43292/Crunchy-Ranch-Pan-fried-Tilapia-Recipe-Is-a-Budget-High-Protein-Dinner-4-Ingredients-15-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 1 cup panko breadcrumbs, or Italian seasoned breadcrumbs
- 1 ounce (1 packet) dry ranch salad dressing mix
- 4 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 1 pound tilapia fillets

## Instructions
1. Combine the breadcrumbs, ranch salad dressing mix, and 3 tablespoons of the olive oil in a small bowl. Mix well. (For a gluten-free version, use gluten-free breadcrumbs or panko. If you don't have Italian-seasoned breadcrumbs, use plain breadcrumbs and add some dried Italian seasoning.)
2. Heat the remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil in a nonstick skillet.
3. Coat the fish with the breadcrumb mixture. Place into the hot oil and cook until golden brown on both sides and cooked through, about 4 to 5 minutes per side. Don't crowd the pan for best results. Serve hot.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Variation: not a fan of tilapia? Try this recipe with your favorite fish, such as cod, haddock, halibut, or even salmon. Add crushed red pepper flakes or ground cayenne for a little spice.
- Serve with a vegetable and a salad for a complete family dinner.
- Nutrition (as provided by the source, per serving): 367 calories, 18 g fat (3 g saturated), 24 g carbs, 1 g fiber, 23 g net carbs, 2 g sugar, 27 g protein, 992 mg sodium, 57 mg cholesterol. The source notes these figures are approximate.

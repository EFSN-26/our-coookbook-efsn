# Buttery Garlic Lemon Shrimp

## Description
A gorgeous-looking but secretly very simple 10-minute dinner — jumbo shrimp seared until pink and curled, then tossed in a buttery garlic-lemon sauce with a kick of crushed red pepper. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/34177/Buttery-Garlic-Lemon-Shrimp-Recipe-Is-Flavor-Fast-10-Minutes-6-Ingredients)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 4 tablespoons clarified butter or ghee, divided
- 1 pound raw jumbo shrimp, peeled and de-veined, tails on if desired
- 6 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- juice of 1 large lemon
- 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- a handful of chopped fresh parsley
- additional lemon wedges, for serving (optional)
- salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Melt half of the clarified butter or ghee in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the shrimp in a single layer and season with salt and pepper. Cook undisturbed for 2 minutes. Flip and continue cooking for 1 to 2 minutes more, until pink, curled and opaque. Set aside on a plate and cover to keep warm. Do not overcook or the shrimp will become tough and rubbery.
2. In the same skillet, add the garlic and cook, stirring continuously, for 30 seconds. Stir in the lemon juice, crushed red pepper flakes and remaining butter/ghee. Return the shrimp to the sauce and toss with the chopped parsley.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- De-veining shrimp is a personal preference — it's not harmful to eat the vein, just cosmetic. Buy pre-cleaned shrimp to save time.
- Serve over rice or quinoa for a complete meal.
- This dish is gluten-free and low-sodium as written.

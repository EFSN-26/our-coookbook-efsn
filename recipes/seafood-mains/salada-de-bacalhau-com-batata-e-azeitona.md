# Salada de bacalhau com batata e azeitona
(Salt Cod Salad with Potato and Olive)

## Description
This salt cod salad with potato and olive is a classic and traditional dish for Brazilian Christmas and New Year's Eve dinners. Simple to make with few ingredients, it is also popular at Easter and can be served warm or cold as a starter or side dish. In this version by cook Juju Guerra, the secret is buying pre-desalted salt cod to simplify the preparation, with a final touch of lemon zest adding a special flavor and delicious aroma. Translated from the original Portuguese source. This is a distinct dish from the cookbook's existing hot bacalhau preparations — it is assembled and served warm or cold as a composed salad rather than a hot baked or sautéed main.

## Ingredients
_(for 10 servings)_

- 800 grams desalted salt cod, in flakes
- 2 onions
- 4 cloves garlic, sliced
- 500 grams small round potatoes (batata bolinha)
- 1 cup sweet grape tomatoes
- 15 black olives
- 100 milliliters olive oil
- Chopped parsley, to taste
- Salt, to taste
- Black pepper, to taste
- Juice and zest of half a Sicilian lemon (or regular lemon)

## Instructions
1. In a pot of boiling water, add the salt cod and cook for approximately 5 minutes to soften. Set aside.
2. In the same water, full of flavor, cook the potatoes until soft.
3. Brown the potatoes in a skillet. Set aside.
4. In the same skillet, add olive oil and sauté the onion until translucent, seasoning with a bit of salt. Set aside.
5. Add more olive oil, sauté the sliced garlic and the cod, and mix well.
6. Add the potatoes, onions, and cod to a serving platter, mix in the halved tomatoes and the olives.
7. Season with olive oil and the lemon juice.
8. Adjust salt and black pepper to taste. Finish with chopped parsley.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
The source suggests adding toasted sliced almonds for a crunchy touch when serving the salad.

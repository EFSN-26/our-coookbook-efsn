# Panko-Crusted Baked Salmon

## Description
Moist, juicy baked salmon topped with a crunchy panko crust loaded with honey mustard, thyme, parsley and smoked paprika — ready in about 20 minutes. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/59163/Crunchy-Top-Baked-Salmon-Recipe-Is-Perfectly-Seasoned-20-Minutes-7-Ingredients)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 4 salmon fillets (about 6 ounces each)
- 2 tablespoons honey mustard
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh thyme, divided
- 2/3 cup panko breadcrumbs
- 4 teaspoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons chopped flat-leaf Italian parsley
- 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
- salt and black pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Stir together the honey mustard and half of the thyme. In another bowl, stir together the panko, remaining thyme, olive oil, parsley and smoked paprika. (Dried thyme can be used if fresh isn't available. For a gluten-free version, use gluten-free panko breadcrumbs.)
2. Season the salmon with salt and black pepper. Put the fillets onto a greased baking sheet, skin side down. Spread the honey mustard mixture on top of the salmon, then sprinkle with the panko mixture.
3. Bake the salmon in a preheated 400-degree F oven for 12 to 15 minutes, or until the fish flakes easily.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- If you don't have honey mustard, any mustard can be substituted, though it will affect the flavor — Dijon adds a wine-y tang, spicy brown mustard adds heat.
- Use gluten-free panko breadcrumbs to make this a gluten-free dinner.
- Serve with a fresh salad and side dishes for a low-carb meal.

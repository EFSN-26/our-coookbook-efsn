# Creamy Garlic Shrimp Recipe Is the Best Thing You'll Eat All Week (20 Minutes, 7 Ingredients)

## Description
Succulent shrimp cooked in a garlicky Parmesan cream sauce, on the table in about 20 minutes. A high-protein, low-carb, gluten-free seafood dinner built from just seven ingredients.

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 pounds jumbo shrimp, peeled, deveined and tails removed
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 5 - 6 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
- 2 tablespoons white wine
- 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
- 1 tablespoon chopped parsley, optional

## Instructions
1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the shrimp and season with salt and pepper. Cook about 2 minutes per side, or until cooked through. Remove to a plate.
2. Add the butter to the skillet. Cook the garlic about 30 seconds. Add the wine and cook, stirring, about 1 minute.
3. Pour in the heavy cream. Add the Parmesan cheese. Stir and bring to a gentle simmer. Reduce the heat to low and cook until thickened, about 2 minutes.
4. Put the shrimp into the sauce. Toss. Garnish with parsley, if desired.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Optional seasoning: Cajun seasoning or Italian seasoning can be added to the shrimp for extra flavor.
- Serve with rice or mashed potatoes to soak up the sauce.
- Store leftovers in an airtight container; reheat quickly in the microwave or over low heat in a saucepot.
- To clean raw shrimp: rinse under cold water, remove the head if present, remove the legs and shell, optionally remove the tail, then run a sharp knife along the surface of the back and rinse out the vein.

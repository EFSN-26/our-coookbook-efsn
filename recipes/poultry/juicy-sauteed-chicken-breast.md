# Juicy Sauteed Chicken Breast

## Description
A fast, one-skillet chicken and vegetable stir-fry — chicken breast strips sautéed with onion and garlic, simmered in a soy sauce-broth mixture thickened with xanthan gum, then finished with bell peppers, broccoli, green onion, and sesame seeds. [Source](https://cookingblast.com/sauteed-chicken/)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 2 pieces chicken breast, cut into strips
- 1 cup broth, your choice of chicken or vegetable
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 3 cloves garlic (approx. 14 g), minced finely
- 1 piece yellow bell pepper, sliced
- 1 piece red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 piece green bell pepper, sliced
- 1/2 head broccoli, broken into small florets
- 1/4 piece green onion, thinly sliced
- 1/2 teaspoon xanthan gum, for thickening
- 1 piece onion (around 50 g), chopped
- 1 dash sesame oil
- 2 tablespoons sesame seeds, for topping

## Instructions
1. Cut the chicken into small pieces and slice all the vegetables, including the broccoli florets and stems.
2. Heat the sesame oil, cook the onion and garlic until soft, then add the chicken and cook about 10 minutes until lightly browned.
3. Season with salt and pepper, add the soy sauce, broth, and xanthan gum, then simmer 5 minutes until slightly thickened.
4. Add the peppers and broccoli, cook until just tender, then finish with the green onions and sesame seeds.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Pound the chicken breast lightly so it cooks evenly and helps avoid dry, overdone meat.
- Season both sides of the chicken before sautéing so the flavor sticks to the chicken instead of the skillet.
- Don't overcrowd the pan, or the chicken will steam instead of sauté.
- Use the browned bits left in the skillet to build a quick pan sauce with a splash of white wine or broth.
- Boneless chicken thighs also work in place of breast for a richer taste with the same seasoning.
- Storage: cool completely before storing in an airtight container in the fridge; label with the date.
- Freezing: freeze in flat portions once completely cooled for faster, more even reheating.
- Nutrition per serving (1 cup, per source): 101 kcal, 16g carbohydrates, 6g protein, 3g fat (0.5g saturated), 5g fiber, 5g sugar, 1074mg sodium.

# Savory Slow Cooker Mediterranean Balsamic Chicken

## Description
Chicken breast slow-cooked with balsamic vinegar, garlic, rosemary, oregano, onions and tomatoes until moist and tender, then finished with fresh spinach — a nine-ingredient, high-protein Mediterranean Diet dinner with only five minutes of prep. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/72099/Savory-Slow-Cooker-Mediterranean-Balsamic-Chicken-Recipe-Is-Full-of-Flavor)

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

- 2 pounds boneless/skinless chicken breast
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 4 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 28 ounces (2 cans) diced tomatoes, with liquid
- 1 cup onion, diced
- 4 teaspoons minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary leaves
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 5 ounces (1 bag) fresh spinach

## Instructions
1. Place the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, diced tomatoes, onions, garlic, rosemary and oregano into the slow cooker. Season with salt and pepper, to taste, if desired. (For best flavor, use a good quality balsamic vinegar — it makes a big difference in taste.)
2. Stir to combine.
3. Place the chicken into the mixture in the slow cooker, ensuring chicken is covered in the mixture.
4. Cover the slow cooker with the lid.
5. Cook on high heat for 3 1/2 hours.
6. Add the spinach to the slow cooker and again cover with lid.
7. Cook for an additional half hour. Chicken is done when it reaches 165 degrees F on a meat thermometer.
8. Stir to combine spinach with rest of the mixture. Serve on its own or over rice or pasta. Garnish with a rosemary sprig, if desired. Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Use a good quality balsamic vinegar for the best flavor — it makes a big difference in taste.
- Serve over rice or pasta to soak up the savory sauce.
- Chicken is done when it reaches 165°F (74°C) on a meat thermometer.

# Easy Broccoli Cashew Chicken Stir Fry

## Description
A Chinese-restaurant-style stir fry made with chicken thighs, crisp broccoli, mushrooms and cashews in a savory-sweet hoisin-soy sauce, ready in 30 minutes. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/45018/Easy-Broccoli-Cashew-Chicken-Stir-Fry-Recipe-30-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 1 1/2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs, trimmed of excess fat and cut into small bite-sized pieces
- 2 teaspoons hoisin sauce
- 1 tablespoon plus 2/3 cup low-sodium soy sauce, divided
- 1/3 cup low-sodium chicken broth
- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 1/2 tablespoons granulated sugar
- 1 tablespoon plus 1 teaspoon rice vinegar (or white wine vinegar)
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil, divided
- 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, optional
- 4 green onions, sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cups broccoli florets, fresh or frozen
- 1 cup sliced mushrooms
- 3/4 cup whole roasted cashews

## Instructions
1. Put the chicken into a bowl. Add the hoisin sauce and 1 tablespoon of soy sauce. Stir to combine then let it sit while you prepare the other ingredients. (Boneless, skinless chicken breasts can be used instead of thighs, but breasts dry out quicker, so be careful not to overcook.)
2. In a small bowl, whisk the 2/3 cup soy sauce, chicken broth, cornstarch, sugar and vinegar. Set aside.
3. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add crushed red pepper flakes to the oil, if using. Once hot, add half the chicken in an even layer and cook for 3 minutes, stirring once or twice. Remove chicken to a plate and repeat with the rest of the chicken.
4. Add remaining oil to skillet. Add broccoli and mushrooms and cook for 2 minutes, stirring frequently. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
5. Return chicken to skillet. Add cashews. Pour in the sauce and cook, stirring often, until the sauce has thickened to your liking.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Other vegetables such as green beans, carrots, spinach, cauliflower or bok choy can be used in place of or alongside the broccoli.
- Serve over rice and garnish with green onions.
- Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge and reheat in the microwave.

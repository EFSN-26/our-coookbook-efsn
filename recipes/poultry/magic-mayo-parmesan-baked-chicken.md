# Magic Mayo Parmesan Baked Chicken

## Description
A three-ingredient shortcut chicken recipe where mayonnaise keeps the meat moist while a Parmesan crust turns golden and creamy in the oven — a fast, high-protein, low-carb weeknight dinner. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/90966/Magic-Mayo-Parmesan-Baked-Chicken-Recipe-Is-Super-Juicy-3-Ingredients-25-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 4 boneless/skinless chicken breasts
- 1/4 cup mayonnaise (the source used Hellmann's Real Mayonnaise)
- 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
- 1/2 to 1 teaspoon parsley flakes or Italian seasoning (optional)
- salt and black pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Season the chicken on both sides with salt and black pepper. Place into a baking dish. (Optional: drizzle the baking dish with a little olive oil first. Boneless/skinless chicken thighs can be used instead of breasts.)
2. In a small bowl, stir together the mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese and seasoning, if using.
3. Spread the mayo mixture over the top of the chicken breasts, coating completely.
4. Bake in a preheated 425-degree F oven until the chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes. (Cooking time depends on the thickness of the chicken and your oven; chicken is done at 165 degrees F on a food thermometer.)
5. Serve. Store leftover chicken in an airtight container and reheat quickly in the microwave.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Boneless/skinless chicken thighs can be substituted for breasts.
- Drizzling the baking dish with a little olive oil before adding the chicken was one preparation the source used.
- Because there's no flour or breading, this recipe is naturally gluten-free.
- Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge; reheat quickly in the microwave.

# Honey Nut Granola

## Description
Sweet, crunchy clusters made from rolled oats, honey and toasted nuts, baked until golden brown. Enjoy as a breakfast cereal or as a topping for yogurt or oatmeal.

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings)_

- 2 cups oats (old-fashioned or quick oats)
- 3/4 cup chopped nuts (this recipe uses a combo of walnuts, cashews and peanuts)
- 1/2 cup shredded unsweetened coconut
- 1/4 teaspoon sea salt
- 1/2 cup almond butter
- 1/2 cup honey
- 1/4 cup maple syrup
- 1/4 cup coconut sugar

## Instructions
1. Heat your oven to 350°F and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Combine the oats, nuts, coconut, and sea salt in a large mixing bowl and set aside.
3. In a small saucepan over medium-low heat, stir together the almond butter, honey, maple syrup, and coconut sugar. Heat and stir until they are melted together and smooth.
4. Pour the almond butter mixture over the oats mixture and stir well.
5. Spread the mixture evenly on your prepared baking sheet and bake for 20 minutes, until golden. Stir every 6-8 minutes to prevent any hot spots from burning.
6. Allow to cool in the pan for 20 minutes.
7. Break into clusters.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Look for certified gluten-free oats on the label if that's a concern.
- Store in an airtight container at room temperature for up to a week.
- Add some spice by sprinkling a teaspoon of cinnamon with the oat mixture before baking.
- Substitute any type of nuts or seeds you like! Pecans, hazelnuts, pistachios, sunflower seeds, and pumpkin seeds are all delicious ideas.

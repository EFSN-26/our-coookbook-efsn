# Creamy Applesauce Oatmeal

## Description
An easy homemade oatmeal made with just four simple ingredients that cooks up on the stovetop in about five minutes — a cozy, apple-flavored breakfast that's food for the soul.

## Ingredients
_(for 1 serving)_

- 1/2 cup milk
- 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
- 1/2 cup applesauce
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon

## Instructions
1. Pour the milk into a saucepot over medium heat.
2. Add the oats, applesauce and cinnamon. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and simmer for about 4 to 5 minutes, stirring often.
3. Remove from heat and serve.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- For nutrition and texture, top the oatmeal with dried fruit, granola or chopped nuts.
- Stir in a drop or two of vanilla extract, if desired.
- For a vegan recipe, use nut milk. Oats are naturally gluten-free, but use gluten-free oats to be sure.
- This recipe can easily be doubled or tripled.
- To make it even tastier, prepare with homemade applesauce.

# Avocado Toast Three Ways

## Description
Crispy garlic-rubbed French toast slices topped with creamy mashed avocado, customizable with three topping options: fried eggs and everything bagel seasoning, balsamic tomatoes and basil, or feta and olives.

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings)_

### Toast
- 1 French loaf, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 garlic clove, whole
- salt and pepper, to taste

### Avocado Mixture
- 4 Hass avocados
- 1 garlic clove, minced
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- salt and pepper, to taste

### Option 1: Fried Egg
- cooking spray
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons Everything Bagel Seasoning

### Option 2: Balsamic Tomato & Basil
- 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 tablespoons fresh basil
- salt, in flakes

### Option 3: Feta & Olives
- 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
- 1/4 cup olives, halved
- 1 teaspoon oregano
- 1 teaspoon olive oil

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Arrange the sliced French loaf on a baking sheet and brush each piece with olive oil. Rub a whole garlic clove across the surface of the bread for added flavor. Lightly season with salt and pepper, then bake for 8-10 minutes until golden and crisp. Remove from the oven and let cool slightly.
2. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Using a fork, mash the avocado to a chunky consistency. Stir in the minced garlic, olive oil, salt and pepper until well combined.
3. Spread the avocado mixture over the toasted bread.
4. **Option 1:** Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly coat with cooking spray. Crack the eggs into the pan and cook until they reach your preferred doneness. Place the eggs on top of the avocado toast and sprinkle with Everything Bagel Seasoning.
5. **Option 2:** Toss the halved cherry tomatoes with balsamic vinegar and a pinch of salt. Spoon over the avocado toast, add fresh basil, and finish with a sprinkle of flaky salt.
6. **Option 3:** Top the avocado toast with crumbled feta cheese and halved olives. Sprinkle with oregano and drizzle a small amount of olive oil over the top before serving.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Choose ripe avocados that are slightly soft when pressed but not mushy.
- Toast the bread until golden brown for a crispy base that resists sogginess.
- Rubbing a whole garlic clove on the bread adds subtle depth without overpowering the avocado.
- Season the avocado mixture well and taste before spreading.
- Additional variations: try smoked salmon, bacon, radishes, red onion, red pepper flakes, or a drizzle of hot honey.
- Assemble right before serving — avocado oxidizes and browns the longer it sits.
- Whole grain bread, sourdough, or bagels can be substituted for the French loaf.
- Storage: store mashed avocado separately in an airtight container with plastic wrap pressed directly onto the surface, up to 24 hours in the fridge. Toasted bread keeps at room temperature in an airtight container. Fully assembled toast does not store well and should not be frozen.

# Flavorful Cilantro Coconut Rice

## Description
Coconut Cilantro Rice is a simple yet flavorful side dish with coconut milk, fresh lime, and a zesty pop of cilantro, that's anything but boring. [Source](https://afoodloverskitchen.com/cilantro-coconut-rice/)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 1 cup (185 g) long grain rice
- 3/4 cup (177 ml) coconut milk
- 1 1/4 cups (296 ml) water
- 1/4 teaspoon (1.5 g) salt
- 1/4 cup (4 g) cilantro, finely chopped
- 1 lime, zested + 1/4 teaspoon juice

## Instructions
1. Combine the rice, coconut milk, water, sugar and salt in a saucepan over medium heat. Cover and bring to a simmer. Reduce heat to medium low and cook for 15 minutes. Remove from heat and let rest for 5 minutes with the lid on.
2. Fluff the rice with a fork. Add the cilantro and stir. Add lime zest and 1/4 teaspoon juice. Stir and serve.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Add the cilantro at the end, after the rice is cooked, to preserve its bright, fresh flavor and vibrant green color.
- Use full-fat coconut milk for a rich, creamy consistency and enhanced coconut flavor; light coconut milk will be less creamy.
- Let the rice rest before fluffing — about 5 minutes with the lid on — to allow the moisture to distribute evenly.

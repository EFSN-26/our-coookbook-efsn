# Turmeric Baked Broccoli

## Description
This tasty turmeric, garlic and lemon baked broccoli recipe is a Mediterranean Diet favorite: fresh broccoli tossed in a bright herb mixture, then baked until crisp-tender. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/65719/Turmeric-Baked-Broccoli-Recipe-Is-Nutritious-Delicious-6-Ingredients-35-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 2 cups fresh broccoli, chopped
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 2 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice (fresh is best)
- 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- salt and pepper, to taste (optional; quantity not specified by source)

## Instructions
1. In a large mixing bowl, combine olive oil, lemon juice, turmeric, garlic powder and oregano. Season with salt and pepper, to taste, if desired. Whisk to combine.
2. Add broccoli to the mixing bowl and stir to coat the broccoli in the mixture.
3. Pour the broccoli into a baking pan or casserole dish (lining with aluminum foil makes for easier cleanup).
4. Bake at 375 degrees F for about 30 minutes, stirring after 15 minutes, until broccoli is crisp-tender.
5. Serve with your favorite protein and side dish. Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
You can use more or less of any seasoning depending on your taste. Fresh lemon juice is preferred, but bottled lemon juice can be used if needed. The source author served this with salmon and a baked potato.

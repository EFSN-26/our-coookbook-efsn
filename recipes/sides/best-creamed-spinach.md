# The Absolute Best Creamed Spinach Recipe Ever

## Description
A wonderfully creamy skillet spinach side dish made with just seven simple ingredients, using either fresh or frozen spinach. Pairs well with chicken, beef, pork, lamb, or seafood.

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 16 ounces frozen chopped spinach, thawed (or 2 pounds fresh spinach)
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
- 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
- 1 pinch nutmeg, ground (optional)

## Instructions
1. If using frozen spinach, press out as much liquid as possible in a mesh strainer. If using fresh, boil a pot of water and cook the spinach for 1 minute, then drain and rinse with cold water and squeeze out the liquid as much as possible.
2. Place a skillet on medium heat with the butter and chopped onion. Sauté for 6 to 7 minutes, or until the onion is soft and a bit brown.
3. Turn heat to medium-low and add the garlic, heavy cream, cream cheese, Parmesan, and the pinch of nutmeg (if using). Keep stirring with a spoon to break up the cream cheese and to melt and blend the cheeses.
4. When the cheeses are blended, stir in the spinach until it's heated through.
5. If the mixture seems too thick, add a bit more cream. If it seems too thin, stir and cook for a few extra minutes until it thickens. Taste and add more salt or pepper as needed (crushed red pepper flakes or ground cayenne can be added for a bit of spice).
6. Serve and enjoy. Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge and reheat in the microwave, adding a splash of heavy cream or milk to loosen it up if needed.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
Store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge and reheat in the microwave; add a splash of heavy cream or milk to loosen it up. Add crushed red pepper flakes or ground cayenne for a bit of spice.

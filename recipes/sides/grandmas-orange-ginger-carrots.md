# Grandma's Orange Ginger Carrots

## Description
A simple, sweet-and-savory glazed carrot side dish passed down in the author's family, made with orange juice, ginger and butter. A Thanksgiving staple that also works alongside chicken, turkey, lamb, beef, pork or seafood any day of the week. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/13111/Grandmas-Orange-Ginger-Carrots-Recipe-7-Ingredients-20-Minutes)

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings)_

- 1 pound carrots, peeled and sliced
- 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1/4 cup orange juice
- 2 tablespoons butter

## Instructions
1. Cook the carrots until tender. Set aside. (Some people don't peel their carrots — if that's you, just wash them before slicing.)
2. Combine the sugar, cornstarch, salt and ginger in a saucepan. Add the orange juice and butter. Cook until slightly thickened. (Pre-squeezed orange juice works, but fresh-squeezed is best if you have time.)
3. Add the carrots. Stir to combine. Garnish with parsley, if desired. Serve.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Source lists cook time as 10 to 15 minutes (total 15 to 20 minutes); this entry uses the upper end of each range.
- Store leftover carrots in an airtight container in the refrigerator and reheat in the microwave.

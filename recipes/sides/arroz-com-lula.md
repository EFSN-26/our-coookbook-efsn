# Arroz com Lula (Rice with Squid)

## Description
A simple, one-pot Brazilian rice dish made with squid rings, onion, garlic, bell pepper and cherry tomatoes — a practical, filling meal for lunch or dinner. [Source](https://receitas.globo.com/tipos-de-prato/acompanhamentos/arroz-com-lula-52151c344d3885234a000041.ghtml)

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings — see §8 for scaling)_

- olive oil, to taste
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 500 g squid, cut into rings
- salt, to taste
- black pepper, to taste
- 1 tablespoon tomato paste
- 1/4 cup green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- 2 cups raw white rice
- 4 cups water
- fresh parsley, chopped, to taste

## Instructions
1. In a wide pot, add a drizzle of olive oil and sauté the onion.
2. Once the onion softens, add the chopped garlic and sauté briefly.
3. Add the squid rings to the pot and season with salt and black pepper to taste.
4. Stir in the tomato paste and mix with the other ingredients.
5. Add the chopped green bell pepper and the halved cherry tomatoes, then add the raw rice. Season with more salt to taste.
6. Carefully pour in the water and cook the rice until the water has been absorbed.
7. Finish with chopped parsley to taste and serve. Bom apetite!

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- This recipe was translated into English from the original Portuguese source (receitas.globo.com); the dish's Portuguese name is retained in the title.
- The source describes this as an easy, one-pot meal, often served around Brazil's "Semana do Pescado" (Seafood Week) in early September.

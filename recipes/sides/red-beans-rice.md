# Easy Red Beans & Rice

## Description
A classic New Orleans dish: sausage, onion and bell pepper simmered with kidney beans, bay leaves and cayenne, then served over rice. This easy Cajun red beans and rice recipe is ready in about 25 minutes. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/16654/Easy-Red-Beans-Rice-Recipe-Is-Louisiana-In-a-Bowl-25-Minutes-High-Fiber-High-Protein)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 pound smoked sausage or andouille sausage, cut into rounds
- 1 onion, chopped
- 1 bell pepper, chopped
- 45 ounces (3 cans) kidney beans, rinsed and drained
- 2 bay leaves
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- ground cayenne, to taste
- 3 cups water
- 2 - 3 cups cooked white rice
- Tabasco sauce, for serving

## Instructions
1. Heat the oil in a large pot. Add the sausage, onion and pepper. Cook until sausage is browned, about 5 minutes. Add the beans, bay leaves, thyme, cayenne and water. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and cook until thickened, about 10 minutes.
2. Remove the bay leaves. Take about one-third of the beans out of the pot and mash with a fork. Return to the pot and stir. Season with salt and pepper. Serve over the cooked rice with Tabasco sauce on the side.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
If you're not a fan of Tabasco sauce, use your favorite bottled hot sauce instead. For a healthier variation, use chicken, turkey or vegan sausage. This recipe reheats beautifully and freezes well — store leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge and reheat in the microwave.

# Roasted Dill Potatoes

## Description
This roasted dill potatoes recipe makes a delicious side dish with some surprising flavors. The method is foolproof: by adding a little water to the roasting pan, you get a tender bite while the skin turns crisp and golden. [Source](https://www.allrecipes.com/roasted-dill-potatoes-recipe-11934600)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 1 1/2 pounds baby yellow potatoes, halved
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper, or to taste
- 3/4 cup water
- 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped, or up to 1/4 cup
- 1/4 cup fresh dill, finely chopped

## Instructions
1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Cut the yellow baby potatoes in half and add them to a baking dish (a 9x9-inch dish works well, but any size you like is fine).
3. Add salt and pepper to taste, and drizzle on the olive oil. Give it a mix, then push the potatoes to one side with a spoon and pour in the water.
4. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven until golden, about 45 minutes.
5. Meanwhile, finely chop the garlic and fresh dill.
6. The moment the potatoes come out of the oven while still sizzling hot, add the garlic and dill right away and give it a mix.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
The residual heat cooks off any raw garlic flavor right away, so add the garlic and dill immediately after the potatoes come out of the oven while they're still sizzling.

# Creamy High-Protein Cottage Cheese Casserole With Spinach & Feta

## Description
A creamy, gluten-free casserole combining cottage cheese, spinach and feta — a delicious Mediterranean Diet combination that's nutritious and a great side dish for dinner, or a light lunch/breakfast. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/64094/Creamy-High-Protein-Cottage-Cheese-Casserole-Recipe-With-Spinach-Feta-9-Ingredients)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1/2 cup sweet onion, diced
- 2 teaspoons garlic, minced
- 1 cup red bell pepper, diced
- 12 ounces fresh baby spinach, washed and dried
- 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
- 2 cups low-fat cottage cheese
- 1/3 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 eggs

## Instructions
1. In a large saucepan, add the olive oil, onion, garlic and pepper. Cook for about 3 minutes. (You can use any color bell pepper you prefer.)
2. Add the spinach and cook until wilted, about 2 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, to taste, if desired. Stir to combine. Remove from heat and drain any excess liquid. Allow to cool for a few minutes.
3. In a large mixing bowl, add the cheeses and the eggs. Season with salt and pepper, to taste, if desired. Stir to combine.
4. Add the vegetable mixture to the cheese mixture. Stir to combine.
5. Pour the combined mixture into an 8x8-inch casserole dish that's been sprayed with cooking spray or lined with aluminum foil.
6. Place baking dish in a preheated 350-degree F oven and bake for about 35 to 40 minutes, or until it's set and cooked through. Dish should be golden brown and solid throughout.
7. Allow to cool for about 10 minutes before serving. Store leftovers in the refrigerator and reheat in the microwave.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Any color bell pepper can be used in place of red.
- Store leftovers in the refrigerator and reheat in the microwave.

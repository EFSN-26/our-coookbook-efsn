# Decadent Boursin Creamed Spinach With Mushrooms

## Description
A decadent creamed spinach recipe where cremini mushrooms add extra flavor and Boursin garlic-and-herb cheese takes it to a new level. Everything cooks in one pan and it's ready in about 15 minutes — a rich, creamy side dish for pasta, grains, potatoes, steak, chicken, pork or seafood.

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 3/4 cup onion, diced
- 8 ounces baby bella cremini mushrooms, sliced
- 10 ounces baby spinach
- 5.3 ounces (1 package) Boursin garlic and fine herb cheese
- 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1/4 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper, or to taste (optional)

## Instructions
1. In a large skillet or saucepan with sides, add the olive oil, onions and mushrooms. Cook until crisp-tender, about 2 minutes.
2. Add the spinach and cook until wilted and integrated, about 2 minutes.
3. Add the Boursin and the heavy cream. Season with salt and pepper, if desired. Stir until the cheese melts and the sauce thickens, about 3 minutes.
4. Serve immediately.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator and reheat in the microwave (add a bit of cream or milk, if needed).

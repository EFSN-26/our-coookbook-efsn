# Craveable Cauliflower Alfredo Casserole With Peas & Bacon

## Description
A quick, gluten-free, four-ingredient cauliflower casserole that makes a wonderful side dish for any simple protein, from chicken to beef to pork to lamb to turkey. It can also be bulked up into a one-dish meal by adding chicken chunks and/or more vegetables.

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 16 ounces cauliflower florets (about 1 medium head of cauliflower)
- 15 ounces (1 jar) alfredo sauce
- 1/2 cup fresh or frozen peas
- 4 strips bacon, cooked and crumbled

## Instructions
1. Cook the bacon in a skillet until cooked and crispy. Remove to a paper towel. Crumble.
2. Cook the cauliflower in boiling, salted water until tender, about 3 to 4 minutes. Don't overcook — you do not want the cauliflower to fall apart. Drain.
3. Put the cauliflower into an 8x8-inch baking dish.
4. Pour the alfredo sauce over the cauliflower. Add the peas and crumbled bacon.
5. Bake in a preheated 425-degree F oven until hot and bubbly, about 30 minutes. Serve.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
For a vegetarian casserole, simply omit the bacon. Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge and reheat in the microwave.

# Garlic Herb Sauteed Mushrooms

## Description
Quick skillet button mushrooms sautéed in olive oil and clarified butter with onion, garlic, white wine (or broth), and fresh parsley, dill and oregano — ready in 10 minutes. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/94934/Garlic-Herb-Sauteed-Mushrooms-Recipe-Is-Nutrient-Dense-Low-In-Calories-10-Minutes-9-Ingredients)

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 pound small button mushrooms, cleaned
- 2 tablespoons clarified butter or ghee
- 1 onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, pressed
- 1/2 cup white wine or broth (chicken broth or vegetable broth)
- small bunch of parsley, chopped
- small bunch of dill weed, chopped
- small bunch of oregano, chopped

## Instructions
1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add in the mushrooms and cook, undisturbed, for 2 to 3 minutes.
2. Add in the butter, and once melted stir in the onion and garlic. Continue to cook for 2 to 3 minutes, stirring frequently.
3. Add in the wine or broth. Cook for about 2 minutes, or until liquid reduces by half.
4. Stir in the herbs and season with sea salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and enjoy immediately.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Recipe and photo courtesy of Clean Food Crush; the source notes the mushrooms keep in the fridge for 2 to 3 days, so they work well for meal prep.
- Great alongside a quick weeknight dinner or as a side for a special holiday meal.
- Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the fridge and reheat in the microwave.

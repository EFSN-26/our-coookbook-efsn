# Lemon Turmeric Broccoli

## Description
A quick, healthy broccoli side dish flavored with refreshing lemon and golden turmeric. It comes together in about 10 minutes and pairs well with chicken, pork, beef, seafood or lamb, or in a grain bowl.

## Ingredients
_(for 4 servings)_

- 3 - 4 cups broccoli florets
- 1/2 - 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice (or to taste)
- olive oil (optional)
- salt, to taste
- black pepper, to taste

## Instructions
1. Put the broccoli and about 1/4 cup of water into a skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until broccoli is crisp-tender, about 2 minutes.
2. Add the turmeric and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper. (You could add in some lemon zest for even more lemon flavor.) Drizzle with a little olive oil, if desired. Continue to cook until broccoli is to desired tenderness, about 3 minutes, stirring often. (For some heat, sprinkle in some crushed red pepper flakes.) Serve.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
Add lemon zest for extra lemon flavor. Sprinkle in crushed red pepper flakes for heat. Delicious in a grain bowl or pasta salad.

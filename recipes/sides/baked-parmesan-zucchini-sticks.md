# Baked Parmesan Zucchini Sticks

## Description
Simple parmesan-coated zucchini sticks, cut, drained, seasoned and baked until golden. A hassle-free way to use up a zucchini harvest, served as a side dish or snack. Note: servings are listed by the source as "40 sticks" rather than a number of people served.

## Ingredients
_(for 40 sticks)_

- 1 zucchini, medium-size
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper, ground
- 1/3 cup parmesan, grated
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions
1. Rinse the zucchini well. Trim off the ends and any bad parts. If the skin is damaged or tough, peel that part off.
2. Cut the zucchini lengthwise into halves, then quarters. You will need thumb-thick, long sticks, so cut them accordingly, trying to make them all the same thickness so they cook evenly in the oven.
3. Place cut sticks into a large mixing bowl and toss with the salt so the salt coats the zucchini; this draws out excess moisture.
4. Leave the zucchini and salt to sit for about 30 minutes at room temperature.
5. Drain and discard the excess liquid that has come out. Pat dry the zucchini sticks with a kitchen paper towel.
6. Preheat the oven to 350°F (180°C).
7. Season the zucchini with the ground black pepper, grated parmesan and olive oil.
8. Toss well so the zucchini is coated properly, being careful not to damage the sticks.
9. Place the zucchini sticks next to each other, with some space in between, on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
10. Bake for about 30-40 minutes at 350°F (180°C), or until they appear a bit golden.
11. Enjoy warm as a side dish or snack.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- You can use small, medium or large zucchini. If using large, overgrown zucchini, cut off any tough, fibrous parts.
- This recipe is prepared without eggs; the zucchini is not "breaded," so the source doesn't call these zucchini fries.
- Optionally flavor further with a few drops of hot sauce, garlic powder or oregano.
- The zucchini sticks are best enjoyed right out of the oven and don't keep well; still, it's better to store and reheat leftovers than discard them, to avoid food waste.
- Serve with sriracha mayonnaise, curried mayonnaise, curry ketchup, ranch dressing or a sour cream dip.
- Nutrition per source (per serving of 5g, i.e. per stick): Calories 10, Fat 1g, Sodium 72mg, Carbohydrates 0.2g, Protein 0.4g.

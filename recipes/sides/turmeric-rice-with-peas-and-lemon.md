# Tasty Turmeric Rice

## Description
A tasty turmeric rice recipe with peas and lemon that's quick, easy and healthy — a great gluten-free side dish with grilled chicken or tofu. [Source](https://30seconds.com/food/tip/16400/Tasty-Turmeric-Rice-Recipe-Is-What-the-Doctor-Ordered-30-Minutes-9-Ingredients)

## Ingredients
_(for 6 servings)_

- 1 cup basmati rice or jasmine rice, rinsed
- 1 cup vegetable broth (you can use chicken broth)
- 1 cup water
- juice of half a lemon
- 1 tablespoon butter (if vegan omit and add 2 tablespoons olive oil)
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon turmeric
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 cup frozen peas, thawed

## Instructions
1. In a pot, bring the broth, water, lemon juice, butter, olive oil, turmeric and salt to a boil. Add the rice. Cover and cook on low for about 20 minutes.
2. Turn off the heat and add the peas and parsley or cilantro. (For color, you could use frozen peas and carrots. Don't like peas? Leave them out!) Taste and re-season, if needed. Cover and allow to sit for about 5 minutes. Stir and serve. Store the rice in an airtight container in the fridge for up to five days.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- For a vegan version, omit the butter and use 2 tablespoons olive oil instead.
- For color, try frozen peas and carrots instead of just peas — or leave the peas out if you don't like them.
- Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to five days.

# Ina Garten's Cauliflower Toast

## Description
Roasted cauliflower mixed with mascarpone, Gruyère, prosciutto, and nutmeg, spooned onto toasted country-style bread and broiled until bubbly — the surprise hit of an all-Ina-Garten-themed "supper club" dinner party described by the author. [Source](https://www.eatingwell.com/ina-garten-cauliflower-toast-appetizer-11766978)

## Ingredients
_(quantities not specified by source — this article is a personal essay describing the dish rather than a formal recipe card)_

- Cauliflower florets
- Olive oil
- Crushed red pepper (chile flakes)
- Salt and pepper
- Mascarpone cheese
- Gruyère cheese, grated
- Prosciutto, thinly sliced
- Ground nutmeg
- Country-style bread, sliced and toasted
- Paprika
- Parmesan cheese, freshly grated, for finishing
- Fresh chives, for finishing
- Flaky salt, for finishing

## Instructions
1. Roast cauliflower florets with olive oil, crushed red pepper, salt, and pepper until tender and browned.
2. In a bowl, mix the roasted cauliflower with mascarpone, Gruyère, thinly sliced prosciutto, and a touch of nutmeg.
3. Spoon the cauliflower mixture onto slices of toasted country-style bread.
4. Sprinkle with paprika and broil until bubbly.
5. Transfer the toasts to a plate and finish with freshly grated Parmesan, chives, and a sprinkle of flaky salt.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- This dish is attributed to Ina Garten and was the standout favorite at an all-Ina-themed dinner party described by the article's author, who was initially skeptical of the ingredient combination (mascarpone, Gruyère, prosciutto, nutmeg) but found it "wildly satisfying."
- The article frames the flavor profile as creamy, earthy, salty, crispy, and sweet all at once.

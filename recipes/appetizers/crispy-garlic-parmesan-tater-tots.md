# Crispy Garlic Parmesan Tater Tots

## Description
Crispy baked tater tots tossed with garlic, Parmesan cheese, and fresh herbs — a four-ingredient upgrade to a frozen-food staple that's ready in about 30 minutes.

## Ingredients
_(for 8 servings)_

- 32 ounces (1 bag) frozen tater tots
- 4 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh flat-leaf Italian parsley, fresh chives or green onion tops

## Instructions
1. Put the tater tots onto a baking sheet. Bake according to package directions.
2. Combine the Parmesan cheese, garlic, and chopped parsley in a small bowl. Set aside.
3. When the tater tots come out of the oven, put them into a large bowl. With a spoon, start stirring and sprinkling in the cheese-garlic mixture. Sprinkle with sea salt and black pepper, if desired. Serve immediately.

## Cook's Notes & Tips
- Try this recipe with sweet potato tater tots.
- Reheat any leftover tots under the broiler for a couple of minutes, watching closely so they don't burn.
